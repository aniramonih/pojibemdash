Does Dairy Queen have a lactose free option?

Yes, Dairy Queen offers a lactose-free option. They have introduced a non-dairy Blizzard that is not only mouthwatering but also caters to those who are lactose intolerant.

What is lactose?

Lactose is a type of sugar found in milk and dairy products. Lactose intolerance occurs when an individual’s body cannot properly break down lactose and can lead to unpleasant symptoms such as bloating, gas, and diarrhea.

What is a Lactose-Free Blizzard?

Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is made with non-dairy soft serve and blended with various mix-ins to create a delicious treat without lactose.

What are the ingredients in Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard?

The non-dairy soft serve used in Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is made from water, sugar, corn syrup, coconut oil, pea protein, and other plant-based ingredients. The mix-ins added vary depending on the Blizzard flavor.

What flavors are available in Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard?

Dairy Queen offers various lactose-free Blizzard options such as Oreo Cookie, Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup, Heath Caramel Brownie, and the popular Girl Scout Thin Mint.

Does Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard contain dairy?

No, Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard does not contain dairy. However, it is made in a facility that also processes milk and dairy products.

Is Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard vegan-friendly?

Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is made without animal-based ingredients, making it a vegan-friendly treat. However, some mix-ins may not be vegan-friendly, so it’s always best to check the ingredients before ordering.

Can I customize my lactose-free Blizzard?

Yes, you can customize your lactose-free Blizzard by choosing your desired mix-in and toppings.

Is there an additional cost for a lactose-free Blizzard?

The price of Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is the same as other Blizzard options.

Are lactose-free Blizzards available at all Dairy Queen locations?

Yes, lactose-free Blizzards are available at all Dairy Queen locations.

Is Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard only available in the United States?

No, Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is available in various countries, including Canada and the United Kingdom.

What other non-dairy options does Dairy Queen offer?

Aside from lactose-free Blizzards, Dairy Queen offers non-dairy Dilly Bars and non-dairy shakes.

Is Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard a healthier option?

Dairy Queen’s lactose-free Blizzard is made with non-dairy soft serve and still contains sugar and fats, making it a treat that should be consumed in moderation.

What are some tips for ordering a lactose-free Blizzard?

When ordering a lactose-free Blizzard, be sure to ask for the non-dairy option and check the mix-in ingredients to ensure they are lactose-free and fit your dietary needs.

Can lactose intolerant individuals consume Dairy Queen’s regular Blizzards?
