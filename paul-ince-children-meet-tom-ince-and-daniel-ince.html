Paul Ince Children : Meet Tom Ince And Daniel Ince

Paul Ince is an English professional football manager and former player who was most recently the manager of Championship side Reading.

In this article, we will bring you details about Paul Ince children Tom Ince and Daniel.

Who is Paul Ince ?

Paul Ince was born on 21 October 1967 in Ilford, London, England. He is 55 years old and stands at a height of 5 ft 10 in (1.78 m).

Paul had a successful spell at clubs such as Manchester United, Inter Milan and others throughout his football career and decided to go into management.

He gained massive attention on 19 February 2022 when he was announced as interim manager of Championship side Reading.

Paul Ince has two children, they are Daniel Ince and Tom Ince, a professional Footballer who plays for Reading in the EFl Championships in England.
