Savannah Clark Guthrie, aka Savannah Guthrie, is a prominent American journalist as well as an attorney by profession. She is widely recognized as the main co-anchor of the NBC News morning show, The Today Show. Not only a host, but she is also the chief legal analyst for NBC News.

The famous anchor, Savannah, was born on 27 December 1971 in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Likewise, she was born under the sun sign, Capricorn. Moreover, her ethnicity is white, and her nationality is American. However, people are more curious to know about personal life. Let's take a sneak peek in her love life.

Relationship Status - Married

The 50 years old anchor, Savannah, is currently a married woman as she tied the knot with Michael Feldman. Well, Michael is a well-known American public relations and communications consultant and a former political advisor. The two of them walked down the aisle on 15 March 2014 and exchanged their wedding vows in front of their close friends and relatives.

The lovey-dovey couple first met at Feldman's 40th birthday party in 2008, where she was with her friend. After their first meeting, they started dating, and later in May 2013, the two went on vacation at Turks and Caicos, where Feldman proposed her. The same month they were engaged. 

As of now, the couple is living happily without any controversies and divorce rumors. Similarly, the pair also shares pictures of them on their respective social media platforms. Recently on 15 March, they celebrated their fifth anniversary and uploaded a photo of them on her Instagram.

Break Up and Proposal The Same Day

It has been four years of their marriage, and still, the couple seems to be sharing a strong bonding. Recently in an interview with Today, Savannah revealed that she broke up with her now-husband, Michael, the day they were engaged. She stated, 

"We did break up on the day that we got engaged."

According to her saying, she parted with Michael in the morning and got engaged in the afternoon. Not to mention, she has had a memorable and fairytale engagement story.

Shares Two Children With Michael Feldman

Not only living happily with each other, but the couple also shares two children from their marital relationship. In an interview with the Today magazine, she said,

"I always wanted to be a mom, and it took us so long to get married — we dated for so long — that we thought maybe it was too late and we'd lost our chance. When we found out, we were pregnant, and I don't think there were two happier people on this planet."

The couple welcomed their first child, a daughter named Vale Guthrie Feldman, in August 2014. As of now, she is attending her elementary school, and her age is five years old. Savannah gave birth to their second child, a son, Charles Max Feldman, in December 2016, and his age is two years old as of now. 

Past Relationship Of Savannah Guthrie

Before tying the knot with Michael, she was in a marital relationship with a English BBC News presenter, Mark Orchard. Mark divorced with his first wife, Anne Kornblut, and started dating Guthrie. Later, they married in December 2005. 

Unfortunately, they decided to part their ways after four years of marriage. To be exact, they officially divorced in 2009. They do not share any kids from their relation.
