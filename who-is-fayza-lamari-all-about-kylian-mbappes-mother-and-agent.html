What does Mbappe’s Mother Do For a Living?

Lamari had a breakthrough in her handball career when she got the opportunity to play for the AS Bondy handball team from the late 1990s to the early 2000s. Even though she wasn’t all that well-liked as a handball player, she received accolades and awards during this time.

After becoming the sports agent for her son Kylian, Fayza experienced a meteoric ascent to stardom. This took place when she decided to put her family first after retiring from her handball career. She utilized her former professional athlete expertise to guide and mentor Kylian and assist him in securing his future career.

Fayza also manages all financial and professional matters about her son’s work as well as decisions regarding his professional advancement. In June 2023, it was revealed that she planned to start her own agency with Kylian as the main representative.
