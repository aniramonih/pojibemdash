Victoria Gotti is an American writer and television personality who is widely recognized as the daughter of the deceased NYC-mafia boss John Gotti. She starred in the reality TV show Growing Up Gotti (2004-2005) and also made memorable guest appearances on the show The Real Housewives of New Jersey in 2013 and 2014. She has released several best-selling books including The Senator’s Daughter (1997), Superstar (2000) and This Family of Mine: What It Was Like Growing Up Gotti (2009).
