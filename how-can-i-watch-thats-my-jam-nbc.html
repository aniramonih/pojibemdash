How can I watch That's My Jam?

NBC'S hit music game show, That's My Jam, returns for its second season in March 2023.

Here's everything to know about how you can watch the new season.

How can I watch That's My Jam?

Season 2 of That's My Jam premieres March 7, 2023.

You can watch the show live on NBC, Tuesdays at 10pm ET.

Episodes are available to stream the next day on Peacock.

Additionally, you can watch That's My Jam on Hulu Live TV, fuboTV, and DIRECTTV STREAM.

What is That's My Jam about?

That's My Jam is a music variety game show that originally aired on November 29, 2021.

The debut episode premiered ahead of its original set release date which was scheduled to be on January 3 of the following year.

The show follows two teams of two celebrities in an episode that competes against each other for a charity of their choice.

In Season 1, fans saw celebrities such as Ariana Grande, Kelly Clarkson, Chance the Rapper, and many more.

The first episode of Season 2 is set to kick off with Jason Derulo and Nicole Scherzinger versus Kelsea Ballerini and Julia Michaels.

Who is the host of That's My Jam?

Late-night talk show host, Jimmy Fallon is set to return as the host of That's My Jam.

The New York native is known to host similar challenges on his show The Tonight Show.

Jimmy Fallon has been a talk show host since 2009 starting off on Late Night with Jimmy Fallon before replacing Jay Leno on The Tonight Show.

The Tonight Show won a Primetime Emmy for Outstanding Creative Achievement in Interactive Media - Social TV Experience in 2015.
