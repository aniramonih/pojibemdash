Will Levis girlfriend Gia Duddy bags a HUGE Burger King deal after going viral for her sexy

Back in April, the Kentucky QB Will Levis went viral on social media after sliding out of the first round in the 2023 NFL Draft. Levis went viral on the Internet for his awkward looks, but his girlfriend Duddy went viral for her “sexy looks.”

And now, Will Levis has revealed that Gia Duddy has signed a deal with Burger King after going viral on Draft Day. Along with Duddy, Will Levis’ sister Kelley Levis also went viral on social media but it was Levis’ girlfriend who took center stage, which led to Duddy doubling her followers count on Instagram and signing a deal with Burger King. “She just signed a Burger King deal. It’s crazy, seriously, I was like, you’re paying for my lunch,” Levis jokingly said. “Her followers doubled on the Draft night and I was like, I need a cut of this.”

The 21-year social media influencer Gia Duddy and Will Levis are the most recent hot couple in the NFL world. Levis and Duddy have been dating for more than two years per reports.
