Mahogany Lox is an American singer, songwriter, DJ, social media star, model, and TV star who is best known for being a briefcase model in Deal or No Deal. She earned her fame from the 6-second videos that she would upload to her Vine account. Following her success on Vine, she started a YouTube channel titled Mahogany Lox to which she uploads videos of challenges, pranks, lifestyle, collabs, reactions, beauty vlogs, and occasional music cover videos. Mahogany is also popularly known for being a descendant of the well-known Gordy family. She has amassed a huge fan base with more than 650k subscribers on YouTube, more than 1.8 million followers on Instagram, more than 1.6 million followers on Twitter, and 100k plus followers on Facebook.

    Mahogany Lox Facts

  • She was born and raised in Michigan.
  • Mahogany’s father is a descendant of the well-known “Motown Gordy Family”.
  • Her parents named her Mahogany after her grandfather Berry Gordy’s film Mahogany (1975). He was also the founder of Motown Records.
  • She was born with a talent for singing, as she descends from a long-line of Motown singers.
  • Mahogany was bullied almost right through her schooling years.
  • She enjoys sketching when she’s not busy.
  • Mahogany is very fond of drinking chocolate milk.
  • She and singer Jacob Whitesides are best friends.
  • In the past, she has been cast in several music videos such as LMFAO’s Party Rock Anthem (2011), Shwayze’s Drunk off your love (2011), and Selfie (2014) by The Chainsmokers.
  • Mahogany’s late aunt Anna Gordy was married to the late Motown singer Marvin Gaye from 1963 to 1977. However, their relationship ended after they legally parted ways.
  • Her older brother Sky Blu and uncle Redfoo formed the well-known musical duo LMFAO.
  • Mahogany is a half-second cousin to President Jimmy Carter. She is a descendant of his great-grandfather James Thomas Gordy’s relationship with a black woman named Esther Johnson.
  • In 2013, she joined the Magcon as a DJ and began touring alongside them. She was the only girl in the group and was also part of their 1st official meet-and-greet event.
  • In 2015, she worked as a correspondent on separate occasions such as during the American Music Awards and in America’s Best Dance Crew. Mahogany also DJ’ed alongside the girl group called Fifth Harmony.
  • She is very fond of her dogs Teddy and Pockets.
