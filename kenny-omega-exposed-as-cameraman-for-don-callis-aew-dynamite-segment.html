Kenny Omega Exposed As Cameraman For Don Callis AEW Dynamite Segment

Kenny Omega is considered one of the best professional wrestles on the planet right now, for numerous reasons. He is currently feuding with The Don Callis family and by the looks of it – Omega was spotted being the cameraman for Don Callis’ segment on AEW Dynamite in Tokyo.

As seen on this week’s episode of AEW Dynamite, Don Callis and Konosuke Takeshita showed a video of them in Tokyo, in the search for Kota Ibushi. They would eventually find Ibushi and brutally beat up him.

However, footage has surfaced which shows that Kenny Omega was the one who filmed the entire segment featuring Don Callis and Konosuke Takeshita in Japan. This may come as a shock to those who believe Omega and Callis hate each other in real life.

Chris Jericho, Kenny Omega and Kota Ibushi are set to square off against Konosuke Takeshita, Sammy Guevara and Will Ospreay in a 3 v 3 tag team match at AEW WrestleDream this week. We’ll have to see who will end up winning the match.
