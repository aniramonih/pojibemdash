Alexander James Caan: Meet James Caans son

Alexander James Caan is the third child of James Caan. Alexander was born on April 10, 1991, to James and his ex-wife, costume designer Linda Stokes. They married in 1995, and by the end of the year, their son James Jr. was born.

Alexander joined his dad for many showbiz events when he was a kid. Now that he’s older, though, he’s taken a step back from the spotlight.

Alex is also a creative person. Alexander has yet to appear in any major TV shows or films, but according to his Instagram, he is an actor.

Alex’s social media accounts reveal that he is also a musician.  On June 16, he launched his new website to showcase his many talents, which include music composition and production, editing, and sound design.

Following his father’s death, he shared a note on his Instagram Story to give fans and friends an update on his family’s whereabouts
