Rahul Suntah Overview and Wiki

Early life

Rahul Suntah‘s journey in music began in the picturesque landscapes of Mauritius. Born on June 6, 1997, he grew up in a culture rich with diverse musical influences. His early exposure to music set the stage for his future successes.

The Genesis: Composing and Self-Taught Prowess

Rahul Suntah’s musical journey took a pivotal turn at the age of 13 when he began composing music. After achieving ABRSM Grade 5, he continued as a self-taught pianist, a testament to his determination and passion for music.

Educational Pursuits: Molding the Music Technologist

Rahul’s thirst for knowledge led him to the University of the West of England in Bristol. Here, he majored in Audio and Music Technology, focusing on composition, production, and sound engineering. This period was marked by a significant achievement – winning the annual UWE Bristol Talent Show in 2019, underscoring his burgeoning talent.

Debut Release

February 2020 marked a milestone in Rahul’s career with the release of his self-composed and produced debut EP, ‘Piano Stories‘. This EP showcased his versatility and creativity, earning him recognition in the musical world.

Rising Fame

Suntah’s flair caught the eyes of many, leading him to the finals of the Mauritian talent show, Zenes Montre To Talan depi Lakaz, in August 2020. His rising trajectory continued as he was spotlighted on BBC Music Introducing in early 2021 for his composition ‘Paradise Island’, which was listed among the top tracks of 2021.

Viral Sensation

Rahul’s adeptness at creating engaging content was evident when his piano covers, particularly Mozart’s Rondo Alla Turca, went viral on Instagram and Facebook. His first major viral success was his 2017 cover of ‘Despacito’, attracting over ten thousand followers in just a few weeks.

Advancing Academia

With a deep interest in cinematography and film music, Rahul pursued a Master’s degree in Film Scoring at the University of Bristol, graduating in 2021. This further enhanced his compositional skills, especially for screen media.

Wider Recognition

Rahul’s rendition of Gorillaz’s ‘Feel Good Inc.’ brought him into the limelight of the American viral video show ‘Right This Minute‘. His album ‘Cinematic Wonders‘, released in December 2021, was a compilation of music written during his university tenure.

February 2022 saw him performing at a prestigious concert in Manchester Cathedral, followed by his first solo concerts in Bristol and London later that year.

Commercial Success and Broadcasting Appearances

Rahul’s cover of ‘I’m So Excited’ by The Pointer Sisters featured in a Dutch recruitment agency’s TV advert. He starred in the commercial and opened for Maandag’s award show. Furthermore, he graced the green room of BBC Radio London, discussing his career and performing live.

Inspiring Collaborations

In a heartwarming move, Rahul collaborated with Rebecca Seziba, a young piano prodigy from Cape Town, in a virtual duet of his composition ‘Home Bound’. This collaboration, supported by actress Viola Davis, showcases Rahul’s commitment to inspiring and supporting emerging talents.

FAQs about Rahul Suntah

Who is Rahul Suntah?

He is a composer, music producer, recording artist, and pianist from Mauritius, currently based in Bristol, England. He has gained popularity through his online presence, showcasing his talents on various platforms.

What is Rahul Suntah’s musical style?

He is known for blending classical and popular music, using extended piano techniques and live loop setups. His style incorporates genres like electronic, jazz, rock, pop, R&B, and EDM. He’s inspired by a range of artists across these genres.

What are some of Suntah’s notable works?

His discography includes the EP Piano Stories (2020), albums Cinematic Wonders (2021) and Super Piano (2023), and the single Horizons (2022). He also has published sheet music arrangements of various songs.

Has he won any awards?

What inspired he to pursue music?

He was classically trained in piano and music theory from a young age and was exposed to various musical styles, which he embraced and incorporated into his work.

What are his future aspirations?
