Justin Flom Bio (Wiki)

Who is Justin Flom?

Justin Flom is an American magician. Justin is popular for his YouTube series and television show Wizards War.

He is famous for his unique brand of magic which makes him one of the most shouted American magicians and the first magicians to focus on social media.

Justin Flom: Birth Age, Family, Education

Justin Flom was born on April 29, 1986, in Las Vegas, Nevada. He is a Taurus.

His father is Scott Flom. He is born into a magician family. His father, as well as his uncle both, whom were a magician.

Justin has three siblings, two brothers, and one sister.

He was homeschooled by his parents. However, he skipped college in 2005.

Justin Flom: Professional Life and Career

Justin performed at The Branson Mall music theater in 2005.

He built a custom-designed theater above the Majestic Steak House in 2006.

Justin starts making more videos in 2011 when fellow magician, Rick Lax, suggested he make more magic videos online.

He started working on new effects with coffee, Coke, Life Savers, and Nerds. Justin posted videos on social media and anywhere else he could find an outlet online.

Justin was in the Band of Magicians with Justin Willman and Nate Staniforth.

He takes charge and became the “heartthrob” of the group. Justin started doing new-school card tricks and even a trick with everyone’s phone and Instagram, and the fans loved it.

He produces a new show, “Wizard Wars” starring along with Penn and Teller and several other magicians competing to create magic out of ordinary objects on the spot.
