Who is Gigi Santo Pietro? All About Vanna White's Daughter

Having such a celebrated actress as a mother, Gigi has consistently been present in the limelight. She has also leveraged her mother’s fame to make a name for herself as a tattoo artist.

How Old Is Gigi Santo Pietro?

Vanna White’s daughter was born Giovanna Santo Pietro on the 2nd of July 1997, making her 26 years old now. Her birth sign is Cancer and she is an American citizen with Caucasian ethnic roots. Giovanna, fondly called Gigi, was born and bred in Los Angeles, California. She is a Christian and attends the Baptist church just like her mother.

Gigi Santo Pietro completed her high school education in 2015 and proceeded to the New York University’s Tich School of the Arts where she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Photography and Imaging in 2019.

While in high school, she was interested in sports and actively participated in gymnastics and soccer training. However, on one of her birthdays, her mother gifted her a camera, after which her interest shifted from sports to photography which she later studied as a course at the university.

Gigi grew up with her older brother, Nicholas, fondly called Nikko. The two siblings have maintained a close relationship over the years. Nikko was born on 10th July 1994, which means he is three years older than Gigi. Nikko is also a college graduate; he attended the University of Arizona and Oregon State University.

What Does Vanna White’s Daughter Do For a Living?

Growing up with her actress mother, Gigi developed an interest in acting as she usually accompanied her mother to her movie sets. She made her TV debut in 2010 in her mother’s popular TV show, Wheel of Fortune, and her mother, Vanna White, is said to have hosted her in the show more than any other person. In 2020, Gigi made an appearance in an episode of CBS News Sunday Morning.

Apart from these two TV appearances, Gigi Santo Pietro has kept her performing skills aside to practice what she studied at the university. She is currently a tattoo artist and is employed at Lincoln Tattoo Company, a tattoo company located in Lincoln, Boulevard, California. The company was founded in 2013 by Paul Friedman and Jason Stores.

Through hard work and dedication, Gigi has recorded a successful career as a tattoo artist. According to her, it is this passion that could keep her up at night. She works very hard to ensure she gives her clients the best tattoo. A glance through her Instagram profile will showcase several of the amazing works she has done over the years.

Gigi has over time stated that she loves what she does and is very proud of her work. Her mother is also proud of her and often celebrates her efforts towards her job.

Gigi’s Father is a Former Hollywood Producer

The name of Gigi Santo Pietro’s father is George Santo Pietro, a native of California. George is a restauranteur, real estate developer, and former Hollywood producer. Some of his movie and TV show production credits include Two and a Half Men, Think Like a Man, The Good Place, The Big Bang Theory, True Blood, Buffy the Vampire Slayers, and L.A. Doctors, among others.

Moreover, George Santo owned and ran a popular restaurant, SantoPietro’s in Bel-Air, California, in a secluded Hollywood strip mall close to Mulholland Drive. The restaurant which was a secret hangout for years was reportedly frequented by Hollywood celebrities in the 1980s. George later opened another restaurant in the same complex called Sushi-ko and it has also remained a very popular restaurant with exclusive customers.

Gigi’s Parents Got Married in 1990 But Are No Longer Together

Gigi Santo Pietro’s mom and dad were once married but not anymore. The couple met for the first time in 1985 in Studio City. Some of Vanna’s friends had dragged her out for a dinner date with George and they clicked at the first meeting. They started dating forthwith and remained in a relationship for the next five years.

They exchanged marital vows in December 1990 after which they lived in their home in Mulholland Drive. Two years into their marriage, White suffered a miscarriage of their first pregnancy and George was very supportive of her through the trying times. They welcomed their first child, Nikko, four years later and seven years later, Gigi joined the family. The couple and their kids enjoyed several happy moments and lived a luxurious life, thanks to their thriving careers.

Their Marriage Lasted For 12 Years

Vanna White and George Pietro filed for divorce in 2002 after 12 years of marriage, citing irreconcilable differences. Gigi was five years old when her parents got divorced and went their separate ways. For the sake of their children, the ex-couple kept their relationship civil and their divorce was finalized without any form of acrimony. Gigi and her brother split their time between mom and dad and still had a normal childhood.

Gigi maintains a close relationship with her family, including her mom who regularly celebrates her, especially on the occasion of her birthday. Gigi once revealed that her mother is the one that instilled in her the secret to lasting happiness and dealing with tough times.

Meanwhile, Gigi Santo Pietro is very private about her personal life.

There are rumors that she has been in a relationship with a man she met in college for three years but there is no verification to that as she herself has never commented on the topic. But as far as the public knows, Gigi is not married and also does not appear to have a child.

Meanwhile, since 2012, Gigi’s mother, Vanna, White has been dating contractor John Donaldson whom she met through a mutual friend. On the other hand, her father, George, has since 8th October 2005 been married to his third wife, Melissa Mascari, and they share a daughter named Chiara, born in 2007.
