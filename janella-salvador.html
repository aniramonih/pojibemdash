Janella Salvador Height, Weight, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Family &amp; More

Janella Salvador (born on March 30, 1998) is a Filipino Singer and Actress based in Cebu, Philippines. She developed an interest in performing arts in her childhood. Janella appeared on stage. In 2008-09, she represented her country in the world fair in Seoul, Korea.

She did her screen debut in 2012s “Be Careful With My Heart” TV show. It rated as the best drama shows aired on the ABS-CBN. She played a young daughter Nikki Grace Lim’s role. Moreover, she became famous for catchy phrases dialogues.

She offered many TV shows such as Maalaala Mo Kaya: Pink Dress, Home Sweetie Home, Born for You, Wansapanataym: Jasmin’s Flower Powers, Maalaala Mo Kaya: White Ribbon, and Maalala Mo Kaya: Cheering Hearts.

As a singer, she released her studio album titled Janella Salvador in the year 2015. She associated with the Star Magic agency.
