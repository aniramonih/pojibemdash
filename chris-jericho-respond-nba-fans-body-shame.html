Chris Jericho Responds To NBA Fans Who Body-Shamed Him By Posting Ripped Selfie

Chris Jericho has again responded to NBA fans who body-shamed him last week. The former AEW World Champion was the victim of fat jokes, attracting criticism from some of the basketball viewers who stayed tuned to TNT after the game between the Milwaukee Bucks and Boston Celtics was over.

Dynamite aired an hour later last Wednesday as a result of the NBA schedule and there was some trickle from the game into Dynamite. Jericho kicked off the show with a match against Top Flight alongside MJF and his appearance triggered lots of negative comments from viewers.

The AEW superstar took to Twitter to respond to his shamers, pointing out he could do more physically, as well as draw record ratings, adding he has really thick skin.

Jericho posted a selfie of himself looking pretty ripped on Monday, captioning it "#FatJericho @NBA." The wrestler has since deleted the post, but not before it was captured by a Reddit user, who has also deleted his post.
