Why cant i pay my bounty in Starfield?

In Starfield, there are specific rules and restrictions when it comes to paying off your bounty. Here are some important things to note:

1. What happens if you have a bounty in the faction space you’re in?

If you have a bounty from the faction space you’re currently in, you cannot clear it at that location. For example, if you have a bounty from the United Colonies (UC), you will need to visit a Bounty clearance terminal in Freestar or Crimson Fleet space to clear it.

2. How do you clear your bounty in Starfield?

To clear your bounty in Starfield, you need to head to the nearest Trackers Alliance Self-Service Bounty Clearance Kiosk. These Kiosks can be found all across the galaxy and are the designated locations for clearing bounties.

3. What should you do if you can’t afford to pay off your bounty?

If you are unable to afford to pay off your Starfield bounty or simply don’t want to, you have the option to allow the guard to take you to jail. The length of your jail sentence will depend on the severity of the crime.

4. Can you clear a Starfield bounty without paying?

No, you cannot clear a Starfield bounty without paying. However, you can choose to go to jail for a few days instead of paying the fine. This will clear your bounty, but you will lose a considerable amount of experience points and any stolen goods will be confiscated.

5. How do you pay a bounty in Starfield without going to jail?

In Starfield, the only way to pay off your bounty is by visiting a Bounty Clearance Kiosk and paying the fine. Going to jail is the alternative if you cannot or do not want to pay the fine.

6. What happens if you go to jail in Starfield?

If you go to jail in Starfield, you will be imprisoned for a certain number of days, depending on the severity of the crimes you have committed. You will also be fined and lose any stolen goods you have. Additionally, you will lose a significant amount of experience points as a penalty.

7. How do you turn yourself in for a bounty in Starfield?

To turn yourself in for a bounty in Starfield, you need to find a Bounty Kiosk located in specific hub locations. In the case of the United Colonies, you can go to New Atlantis and visit the Viewport Bar to turn yourself in. Keep in mind the faction affiliations before charting your grav jump.

8. Where do you pay your bounty in Jemison Starfield?

In Jemison, the city of New Atlantis has a Bounty Clearance Kiosk called the Viewport Bar. This is where you can go to pay off your bounty in Jemison.

9. Can you be a bounty hunter in Starfield?

Yes, you can be a bounty hunter in Starfield. The Trackers Alliance is the guild for bounty hunters in the game. You can take on bounty hunting missions from the Trackers Alliance at certain kiosks in populated areas.

Having a bounty in Starfield can lead to attacks if you attempt to land on a planet controlled by the offended faction. It is recommended to avoid landing on such planets to prevent confrontation.
