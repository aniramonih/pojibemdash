Chris Diamantopoulos is a Canadian actor, voice actor, and stage artist. He is known for playing Russ Hanneman in the comedy series Silicon Valley, Evan Phinnaeus ‘Finn’ Woodhouse in the period drama series Good Girls Revolt, Rodney in the miniseries The Starter Wife, Rob Weiss in the action drama series 24, Moe Howard in the slapstick comedy film The Three Stooges, Barry Cross in the sitcom Let’s Get Physical, and Brian in the mockumentary sitcom The Office. In addition to that, Chris has also been cast in several other projects like The Wonderful World of Mickey Mouse, Harley Quinn, The Harper House, Blood of Zeus, BoJack Horseman, Made in Hollywood, Arrested Development, Boston Legal, American Dad!, American Dreams, Law & Order, Batman Unlimited: Animal Instincts, Batman Unlimited: Monster Mayhem, Batman Unlimited: Mechs vs. Mutants, Three Days to Vegas, Empire State, The Art of the Steal, and Diary of a Wimpy Kid.

  • He was raised dividing his time between Canada and Greece.
