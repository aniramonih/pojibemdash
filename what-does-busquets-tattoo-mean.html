What does Busquets tattoo mean?

Sergio Busquets is a renowned Spanish professional footballer who captains and plays as a defensive midfielder for La Liga club Barcelona and the Spain national team.

Sergio Busquets Burgos was born on July 16, 1988, in Sabadell, Spain. His father, Carles, was also a footballer and had played as a goalkeeper for Barcelona during the 1990s.

Sergio Busquets made his senior international debut for Spain in April 2009. He has since made over 130 appearances for the national side and aided Spain in winning the 2010 World Cup and Euro 2012 tournaments.

Sergio Busquets is presently in a relationship with Elena Galera whom he began dating in 2014. The pair have two sons born in 2016 and 2018.

What does Busquets tattoo mean?

Sergio Busquets has an Arabic tattoo on his left forearm which translates to “A thing for you, the life in my country”. The tattoo is dedicated to his maternal grandfather whom he was close to.
