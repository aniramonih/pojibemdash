Arsema Thomas Bio, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth, Wiki

Arsema Thomas is an American female actor widely famous for her role as Young Lady Danbury in the Netflix series Queen Charlotte (2023). Her journey from working as a butcher’s assistant to a rising star in the entertainment industry is certainly inspiring. She wasn’t allowed to use the phone in front of customers while working but she knew that one day her life would be changed. 

Early Life

Arsema Thomas was born on 19th June 1994 in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. She is the daughter of Adebisi Babatunde Thomas (a Nigerian father) and Nardos Bekele-Thomas (an Ethiopian mother). Her parents moved to Kampala, Uganda, where she spent her childhood. Her father died due to Lou Gehrig’s disease. She has a sister named Abigail Thomas. 

Regarding her education, Arsema finished her primary education at Linden Hall (Boarding School) in Lititz, Lancaster, Pennsylvania. Later, she earned a Bachelor of Arts in Biophysics from Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in 2016. After that, she earned a Master of Public Health degree from Yale University in 2018. While studying at University, she worked as an administrative assistant and performed the task.

Acting 

Arsema Thomas began her interest in acting and entertainment. Initially, she attended short programs courses at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, the Cours Florent in Paris, and the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) in London. In 2018, She got her first role as Leila in Nightwatcher short film. Later, she was first recognized for her role as Christie in a 2021 episode of One Touch.

Through her dedication, she graduated with a Master of Fine Arts in Professional Acting from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) in 2022. She gave auditions and got the opportunity to play the role of Rebecca in the film Redeeming Love (Released on 21st January 2022). She is currently involved in the Netflix series “Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story.”

In the series, she portrayed her acting skills with the distinct role of Young Agatha Danbury. She played the role alongside other famous male and female actors like India Amarteifio (who portrayed Young Queen Charlotte) and Corey Mylchreest (who portrayed Young King George).
