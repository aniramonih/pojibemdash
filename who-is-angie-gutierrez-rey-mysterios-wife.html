How did Rey Mysterio Meet His Wife?

Rey Mysterio and his wife met, Angie Gutierrez met as teenagers in a gym in the early 1990s. At the time, Oscar Gutierrez, who is more famously known as Rey Mysterio, had already started his career as a professional wrestler, although he was not very famous.
