Inside The Life Of Melisa Asl Pamuk-The winner of Miss Turkey 2011

Melisa Aslı Pamuk is a Turkish citizen renowned as a model. She has made her identity as the winner of Miss Turkey 2011. Furthermore, she is also known for her acting in Her Sevda Bir Veda, Ulan Istanbul, Endless love, Yeni Hayat, and more.

Pamuk was born on 14th April 1991 in Haarlem, Netherlands. She is 5 ft and 8 inches (176 cm) tall and has a weight of 56 kgs (124 lbs). 

Career outset of Pamuk

Pamuk began her modeling career at the age of 14. But, she was acting from the age of 13. Regarding her modeling vocation, she participated in various beauty pageant competitions and has worked with different brands as well.

Talking about her acting career, she debuted from a short movie, Dat zit well snor, in 2004. later, she came back on screen in G.D.O. BlackCat in 2013.

IMDb Profile of Pamuk

Pamuk has earned thirteen credits on IMDb as an actress. She has acted in ten TV series, one short movie, and two movies.

More, she has also added awards and nominations in her name. According to her IMDb profile, she has the star sign, Aries.

Awards & Nominations

Pamuk has added two awards and two nominations to her IMDb profile. She is the winner of the 2016 Pantene Golden Butterfly Award which she received for Rising Star. She was also honored by Footed Newspaper TV Stars Award in 2017 for Kara Sevda.

In 2019, she was nominated for Golden Star by Turkey Youth Awards for Carpisma. Recently, in 2021, she was nominated by Ayakli Gazete TV Stars Awards for the Best Actress in Kilit.

Where Did Pamuk Study?

Pamuk completed her high school in Haarlem and later attended the University of Amsterdam. She studied Psychology there. 

However, Pamuk dropped out the university for her passion for modeling. She started working as a model and even went to different modeling competitions.

Net Worth of Melisa Aslı Pamuk

Pamuk has earned a hefty sum of money from her modeling and acting career. She has a net worth of $2 million ensued from her works in the film industry.

Further, her passive sources of income include the income from her social media and that from her YouTube videos.

Pamuk's Parents

Pamuk was raised by her parents in Harlem. Her father's name is Kadir Pamuk. However, she has not revealed her mother's name. Her parents belong to Iskenderun, Hatay, Turkey.

Pamuk is very close to her parents and other family members. She often visits her parents whenever she is free.

Siblings of Pamuk

Pamuk has a brother. His name is Kaan Pamuk. The brother and sister share many photos on their Instagram profile.

Melisa and Kaan are more like friends rather than siblings. They are very helpful to each other and support one another greatly.

Who is Melisa Dating?

Melisa is not dating anyone at present. However, she was dating Tolga Sala from late 2012 through May 2019.

Soon, she dated Oğuzhan Özyakup but things did not go right, and they finally split in June 2021.

Pet Lover Melisa Aslı Pamuk

Pamuk is a cat-friendly model. She shares many pictures of her pet on her Instagram. She cares for her pet immensely.

Further, she also respects animal rights that encourages her fans to love animals.
