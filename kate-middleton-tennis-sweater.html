Kate Middleton Matched Her Sweater to Her Shoes in a Preppy Tennis Look

Tennis, anyone?

On the last day of their Scotland tour, Kate Middleton and Prince William stopped by the Craiglockhart Tennis Centre in Edinburgh for a tennis match with children in the Lawn Tennis Association's youth program.

The Duchess of Cambridge looked the part of off-duty tennis champ in a V-neck off-white cable knit sweater with navy and red piping paired with a set of black capri pants and sporty white Superga sneakers.

The Duke of Cambridge looked a tad less tennis-ready in a blue-gray blazer layered over a sweater and a button-up.

Though away from her children, Middleton kept George, 7, Charlotte, 6, and Louis, 3, close by with a gold necklace bearing each of her kids' first initials on a chain.

The Cambridges split into teams (Team Duke and Team Duchess) for a match with the youth tennis players. According to the couple's Instagram, Team Duke was "the clear winner" … Hmm, is Prince William running his family's social media now?
