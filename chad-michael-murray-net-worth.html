Chad Michael Murray Net Worth

What is Chad Michael Murray’s net worth

Chad Michael Murray is an American actor, model, and spokesperson with a net worth of $4 million. He gained his wealth through his work as a fashion model for popular brands and his acting career in both TV and movies. Murray is best known for his role in the WB’s “One Tree Hill.”

Early Life and Personal Struggles

Chad Michael Murray was born in Buffalo, New York in 1981. He attended Clarence High School where he played football and developed a love for reading. Murray’s mother abandoned him when he was a baby, leaving his father, an air traffic controller, to raise him.

During his late teens, Murray was attacked by three men at a Burger King, resulting in a broken nose. This led to rumors that he had undergone a nose job, but it was simply reset by emergency room doctors after the incident.

Chad Michael Murray’s Acting Career

Chad Michael Murray began his acting career in 2000, playing the role of Tristan DuGrey in the TV series “Gilmore Girls.” He then appeared in several guest-starring roles and played Charlie Todd in “Dawson’s Creek.” In 2003, he landed his breakthrough role as Lucas Scott in “One Tree Hill,” which became the most successful and top-rated TV program for The WB at the time.

Following the success of “One Tree Hill,” Murray starred in the romantic comedy “A Cinderella Story” and the horror film “House of Wax.” He also appeared in “Home of the Brave” and made a guest appearance in the final season of “One Tree Hill.”

In 2014, Murray was cast in a featured role as Agent Jack Thompson for Marvel’s TV series “Agent Carter,” and returned for its second season. He also appeared in “Fruitvale Station” and was cast in a recurring role as Edgar Evernever on the CW drama “Riverdale.” In addition to his television work, Murray has starred in several Hallmark Christmas movies and is set to portray Ted Bundy in “American Boogeyman.”

Chad Michael Murray’s Career Highlights

Chad Michael Murray’s popularity as an actor and heartthrob led to lucrative endorsement deals with MasterCard, K-Mart, Cingular Wireless, and Chevy Cobalt. He has also graced the covers of Rolling Stone and People magazines.

In addition to his acting career, Murray is also a published author. He released his first novel, “Everlast,” in 2011. He later co-authored a romantic thriller, “American Drifter: An Exhilarating Tale of Love and Murder,” which was said to be inspired by one of his dreams, with Heather Graham in 2017.

Personal Life

Chad Michael Murray married co-star Sophia Bush in April 2005, but the couple separated just five months later and divorced in December 2006. He then dated co-star Nicky Whelan and was engaged to Kenzie Dalton, whom he met on the set of “One Tree Hill.” Murray and Dalton were engaged in April 2006 but called off their seven-year engagement in August 2014. Murray married his “Chosen” co-star Sarah Roemer in 2015, and they have two children together – a son born in 2015 and a daughter born in 2017.
