William Pedranti Is The Co-Founder Of PENG Life

Jennifer Pedranti husband William Pedranti is the Co-Founder Of PENG Life. During his career, he has co-founded many biotech businesses.

PENG Life Science Venture is a financial consulting located in Orange, California. The firm helps in collaborations, purchases, capital advisory, and consulting.

According to William’s LinkedIn profile, he started his career with Latham & Watkins LLP in 2000. He was an Attorney for about 3 years and 7 months before joining Spectrum Pharmaceuticals in 2004.

Later joined Spectrum Pharmaceutical as Vice President and General Counsel. He was also a member of the Executive Committee. He worked in the firm for 6 years and 3 months.

He also worked with Snell & Wilmer, Hall Estill, Scilex Pharmecuticals, Inc., Inflammatory Response Research, Inc, and many other companies.
