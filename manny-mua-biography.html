Manny MUA Bio (Wiki)

Who is Manny MUA?

American Manny MUA is a make-up artist and beauty influencer. He is renowned for his beauty content that went viral on YouTube and Instagram.

Manny is also the first male band ambassador of Maybelline. Also, he is the founder of the cosmetics brand, Lunar Beauty.

Who are the parents of Manny MUA?

Manny MUA was born Manuel Gutierrez on the 4th of April 1991 in San Diego, California.

His father, Manny Gutierrez Sr., and mother, Greka Gutierrez have often been featured on his YouTube channel. They are of British-Irish ethnicity.

Manny MUA is the eldest among his two siblings; Nick and Aaron Gutierrez.

Talking about their educational background, he graduated from San Diego State University with Honors in Health science.

Initially, he planned to go to medical school and became a plastic surgeon. However, he started exploring social media as a beauty influencer in his career.

Manny MUA- Professional Career

Before being a YouTube personality, Manny MUA started his career as a makeup artist in the salons of San Diego including the Sephora and MAC. 

Meanwhile, Manny MUA became a friend of the Instagram star, Patrick Simonds. Along with the guidance of Patrick, he started posting on Instagram.

In 2014, he created his own YouTube channel for sharing his beauty tips, tricks, and many more. With his content, in 2017, he earned around 2 million subscribers on the channel.

Also, he got extremely popular on Instagram too. The same year, he was announced as the brand ambassador of Maybelline making him the first male ambassador for the brand.

Also, he was chosen to be a spokesperson for the brand making him the second among the male after, James Charles.

Moving forwards, the following year, he launched his beauty brand, Lunar Beauty. Also, Forbes named him as 30 under 30 in the Arts and Styles category.

Manny MUA: Net Worth, Salary

As of 2020, Manny MUA has an estimated net worth of $2 million. Through his YouTube channel, he makes in the range of $16.8k to $268.3k. Besides that, as a brand ambassador, his earnings are $22k to $66k.

Apart from that, he also makes through brand promotion via his YouTube and Instagram accounts.

Also, his net worth has significant contributions via his cosmetics brand, Lunar Beauty. As of now, he is expanding his career with the brand as a beauty influencer.

Is Manny MUA involved in any rumors and controversies?

Back, in 2017, Black Moon Cosmetics sued him and his friend, Jeffree Star for copyright and trademark infringement.

Before that, the duo announced a collaboration with the star’s makeup brand. Black Moon accused them of using its logo with permission.

Later. the matter with resolved without any buzz in the media.
