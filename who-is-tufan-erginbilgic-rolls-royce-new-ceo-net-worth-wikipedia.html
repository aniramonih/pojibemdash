What’s Tufan Erginbilgic’s name? Wikipedia: The New CEO of Rolls-Royce

Tufan Erginbilgic, a former executive at BP and an engineer, will take over as CEO of Rolls Royce from Warren East at the beginning of next year.

Tufan is an engineer by trade. He has worked for BP for more than 20 years, including five years as a member of the executive team.

Since he started working for BP in 1997, he has been in charge of the company’s downstream business. This includes refining, petrochemicals, the service station network, lubricants, midstream activities, and the Air BP jet fuel division. He will leave the company in 2020.

Tufan Erginbilgic is currently a non-executive director of the energy company Türkiye Petrol Rafinerileri A., the energy, healthcare, and technology company DCC plc, and the worldwide transport vehicle maker Iveco Group NV.

Also, as a partner at Global Infrastructure Partners (GIP), a private equity firm that specializes in large-scale investments in infrastructure firms, Tufan is in charge of $81 billion for investors.
