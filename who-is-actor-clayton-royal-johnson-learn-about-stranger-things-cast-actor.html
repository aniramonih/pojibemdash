Who is Actor Clayton Johnson? Wiki And Bio

Clayton Johnson was born in Leesburg, Virginia. In 2009, he moved to Orlando, Florida, with his family. 

He is an actor known for Nickelodeon’s I Am Frankie, The Onion Movie (2018), and the TV mini-series Dreamfactory (2018). He was also involved in his local children’s theater, where he played Anthony in Sweeney Todd, Tarzan in Tarzan, and Young Ebenezer in A Christmas Carol.

He is also a classical vocalist, a drummer, and a tapper. He’s a 3rd-degree black belt in Taekwondo, a green belt in Krav Maga, and a nationally ranked archer. 

He has been part of more than ten movies. He has played the main lead in the short drama movie Caleb (2018). He has been cast in movies like Crescent City Chronicles: CHains in the Attic, Surviving Lunch, Stranger Things (Season 2), My Best Friend’s Exorcism, and Family Camp.

He first appeared in the short movie “Master Exploder” as Rob. In 2018, he appeared in five shows, three of which were short movies, one was TV series, and one was a movie named “Crescent City Chronicles: Chains in the Attic.”

However, in 2009, he appeared only in one movie, “Surviving Lunch”, and in 2020, he was seen in two shows, “The DreamFactory” and “Rock-a-Bye.”

In 2021 he has been featured in “Lennox and the Dark Lord’s Shadow” as Lennox. Furthermore, in 2022, he appeared in Family Camp, a comedy film as Corbin; he appeared in Stranger Things as Andy. He is also cast in the Wildflower movie, which is in the post-production stage.
