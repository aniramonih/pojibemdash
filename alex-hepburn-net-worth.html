What is Alex Hepburn's Net Worth?

Alex Hepburn is a British singer and songwriter who has a net worth of $5 million. Alex Hepburn was born in London, England in December 1986. Hepburn has been compared to Janis Joplin and Amy Winehouse. Her family moved to Valbonne, France in 1995. At 16 years old Alex left school to pursue music. Her father passed away when she was 18 years old. She became popular after performing at a Bruno Mars concert.

She never took singing lessons. Hepburn's debut studio albums Together Alone was released in 2013. The album peaked at #2 in Switzerland, #3 in France, #15 in Belgium, #23 in Germany, and #36 in Austria. The album contained the singles "Under", which peaked at #1 in the Czech Republic and #2 in France, Poland, and Belgium. The album's single "Miss Misery" also made the top 100 in Belgium, Poland, and Switzerland. Alex is signed to Warner Music France. Her Together Alone tour started in Belgium in March 2014.
