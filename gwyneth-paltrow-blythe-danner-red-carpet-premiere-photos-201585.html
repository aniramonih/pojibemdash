Gwyneth Paltrow Supports Mom Blythe Danner on Red Carpet: Photos

Too sweet!

Just three days before Mother’s Day 2015, Gwyneth Paltrow stepped out to attend the L.A. premiere of mom Blythe Danner’s new film, I’ll See You in My Dreams, and the duo could not have looked sweeter on the red carpet!

Holding on to her mom’s waist, Paltrow, 42, posed for photos in a sleeveless white Creatures of the Wind minidress with a turtleneck collar and a pleated skirt, which the Mortdecai stunner paired with black ankle-strap sandals. Her flaxen tresses were styled straight with a slight wave, and she added peach cheeks and a rose-hued lip.

Meanwhile, Danner, 72, who took a more boho approach, gussied up in a zebra-print top, teamed with a floral-print midi skirt, black boots, and silver jewels. She was snapped hugging her daughter throughout the night, on the red carpet and inside the screening at The London West Hollywood hotel.

It was a low-key affair for both ladies—unlike the Costume Institute Gala 2015, which took place earlier this week in NYC. While Paltrow has slayed the Gala carpet in years past, the actress decided to stay home for the 2015 event and have an Instagram session with her daughter, Apple, 10, instead. Captioning a cute shot of the pre-teen in dino feet the next day, Paltrow wrote, “This was the red carpet in my house last night.”
