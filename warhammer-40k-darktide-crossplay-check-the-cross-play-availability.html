Warhammer 40k Darktide Crossplay: Check the Cross-play Availability

Exploring Warhammer 40K Darktide Crossplay Feature

Confirmation has been made that Warhammer 40,000: Darktide now supports crossplay between the Steam and Microsoft Store editions. This development was introduced with Patch 11 on June 28, 2023, allowing players from both platforms to team up and combat plague cultists together. The integration of internal cross-network servers facilitates seamless matchmaking, adding friends, and participating in games across both storefronts.

The inclusion of crossplay marks a positive move for the community, as it unites a larger player base and enables friends to play jointly, irrespective of the platform on which they possess the game. It is worth noting, however, that crossplay does not incorporate cross-progression, preventing players from transferring their progress between different platforms. Withal, the incorporation of crossplay significantly enhances the multiplayer experience, fostering a more united and vibrant player community for Warhammer 40K: Darktide.
