Nathan Parsons Wife, Girlfriend, Family, Net Worth

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: October 22, 2019

Australian actor Nathan Parsons dreamed of being a Broadway dancer, at a young age.

But as he grew up, he got bitten by the acting bug and hit the roads to Los Angeles after being accepted into the University of Southern California under the BFA acting program.

Nathan made his first television debut as Ethan Lovett on daytime soap opera General Hospital and ended up being a household name.

Short- Bio, Family

Nathan Parsons was born as Nathan Dean Parsons, in Adelaide, Australia on 16 June 1988. Nathan grew up in a family of five alongside his two brothers- Robert Parsons and Jared Parsons. Although not much is known about his parents, his father worked as a civilian supervisor at a military base.

Nathan's family moved to the United States when he was very young. At first, they relocated to Colorado but eventually settled in Texas. And it was in Texas, where Nathan completed his high school at St. Andrew's Episcopal School.

After graduating high school, Nathan attended the University of Southern California. But unfortunately, he dropped out of college after a year and a half as he was too eager to test the waters of the real world. 

Career, Net Worth

After dropping out of college without graduation, it was not a steady ride to fame for Nathan. He made his first screen appearance in the 2007 horror-comedy Teeth. After many minor roles in TV shows, he finally got his big break in 2009 from General Hospital.

After that, he has appeared in the hit vampire series True Blood and The Originals. Further, he also had a recurring role on the ABC Family series, Bunheads.

The good looking actor of height 6 feet (1.83 m) for sure has made a commendable career in acting. In addition to his handsome face and masculine built, Nathan also has an exciting feather tattoo on his biceps.

As for his net worth, with an average actor's salary of $50,000 per year, Nathan undoubtedly boasts a decent wealth. Moreover, with new projects like Lionsgate/Kingdom Studios’ faith-based drama, I Still Believe in his kitty, Nathan's net worth is more likely to increase.
