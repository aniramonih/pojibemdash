Danny Trejo Net Worth: Books &amp; Charity

Danny Trejo, an American film, television actor, and restaurateur, has a net worth of $8 million.

Dan Trejo was born on May 16, 1944, in Echo Park, Los Angeles, to Alice Rivera and Dionisio “Dan” Trejo.

Trejo went in and out of jail and prison in California throughout the 1960s.

He began boxing while serving a sentence in San Quentin and became a champion fighter in the prison’s lightweight and welterweight divisions.

Trejo is noted for his unique appearance, which includes scars on his face, long hair, a beard, and numerous tattoos.

He has appeared in over seventy different television shows since 1990, in addition to his film career.

Trejo is frequently typecast as a rugged, thuggish character in film and television, but he has also appeared in more sympathetic roles, such as “Spy Kids,” “Bubble Boy,” and “Monk.”

Moreover, on March 8th, 2012, he was inducted into the ‘Texas Film Hall of Fame.‘

In 2014, Trejo produced his first film, “Ambition,” followed by “Bad Asses” the following year.

‘Tough Guy’ Trejo was also awarded the ‘Best Picture Trophy’ at the ‘United International Film Festival’ for ‘The Weight of Blood and Bones’ in 2016.

Being a successful restauranteur he is, his rainbow cauliflower tacos were named one of the top ten recipes of 2017 by the Los Angeles Times.

What happened to Danny Trejo’s “Machete” face?
