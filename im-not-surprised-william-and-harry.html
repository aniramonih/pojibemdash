I'm not surprised William and Harry didn't meet up

Hi Royal Clubbers,

To say it's been quite the week in the world of royal reporting would be an understatement.

One of the headlines flying around was that Prince William "refused" to see Prince Harry when he was in the UK this week, and that Harry would have been "willing" to patch things up with his big brother. But did Harry even extend that olive branch in the first place? It's arguable.

Share

What we do know for sure is that this was a very short visit. Harry was on home soil for around 25 hours, and his sole focus was seeing his father, King Charles. Under such time constraints, meeting with William too would have been tough. But there are other factors, beyond the logistical, that made a brotherly reunion this week a long shot.
