Buenos Dias memes

Buenos Dias is the Spanish word for Good Morning. Well, it is always nice to have an excellent start to your morning. As we all know, a good morning makes for a good day. No doubt, our day feels much more pleasant when we wake up in a happy mood.

However, it doesn’t always feel so fresh and pleasant to wake up early in the morning. Especially a cold Monday morning when we have to start the same mundane routine. We feel you! All of us have been there.

What is funnier are movies that show people waking up with a happy smile and hair and face so fresh that it makes us wonder if that is even remotely possible. But, of course, we don’t think that could ever happen because all we get when we get out of our soft, comfortable bed is a grumpy face and unmanageable frizzy hair. If only Hollywood made one realistic morning routine movie!

So, tell us what wakes you up and transforms you from a zombie into a fully functioning human? Is it a cup of strong coffee or the cardio exercise you do religiously every morning? Whatever it is, we hope it makes your morning a good morning!

After all, no matter how often we snooze our alarms, we have to leave that warm bed and start our day. Okay, we agree it’s not easy. We know, we even question ourselves if sacrificing our sleep for whatever we are doing is worth it. But believe us, it is worth it. So, we have got to win this tough battle for a happy, fruitful day.
