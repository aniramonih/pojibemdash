Who Are Robin Thicke's Parents? Age, Wife, Girlfriend, Children

Robin Thicke, whose full name is Robin Alan Thicke, is a well-known singer, songwriter, and record producer from the United States who became famous after the release of one of the best-selling singles of all time called “Blurred Lines” released in 2013. He is known to have worked with several artists including K. Michelle, Pharrell, Jessie J, Christina Aguilera, etc.

He is in news recently for giving tribute to his late father by performing on the theme song of “Growing Pains” called “As Long As We Got Each  Other” on the Wednesday, 5th October 2022 episode of the show “The Masked Singer” where he is the judge.

Who Are Robin Thicke’s Parents?

Thicke was born to Alan Thicke (father) and his then-wife Gloria Loring (mother). His father was a Canadian actor and his mother is an American Actress and singer. His parents got divorced around seven years after he was born. He has an older brother named Brennan Thicke who is a voice actor.

Robin Thicke Age

Thicke is 45 years old as of 2022, he was born on 10th March 1977 in Los Angeles, California, United States. His zodiac sign is Pisces and he is a follower of Christianity.

Robin Thicke Wife

Thicke was married twice, his first wife’s name is Paula Patton. She is an American actress and producer. They met when they were still teenagers, and later got married in the year 2005. They got divorced in March 2015.

Robin Thicke Girlfriend

Thicke is currently in a relationship with April Love Geary whom he met at a party in 2014. They got engaged in December 2018.

Robin Thicke Children

Thicke is the father of four kids, including a son named Julian Fuego Thicke from his first wife born in April 2010. He has three other children born with his second wife, a daughter named Mia Love Thicke born in February 2018, their second daughter named Lola Alain Thicke was born in February 2019, and their son named Luca Patrick Thicke was born in December 2020.
