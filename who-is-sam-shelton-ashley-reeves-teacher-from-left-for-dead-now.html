Who Is Sam Shelton? Ashley Reeves Teacher From Left For Dead Now

Where Is Sam Shelton Now? Sam is currently at Hill Correctional Center in Galesburg. Find out the wiki details of the teacher whose story is portrayed in Left for Dead.

Sam, aka Samson Shelton’s story, is now in the movie.

Lifetime has summed up Ashley Reeves and his case into a motion picture bringing back all the stories and insights to the light. Sam pleaded guilty to attempted murder against Reeves, who was his student.

Where Is Sam Shelton Now?

Sam Shelton is incarcerated at the medium-security Hill Correctional Center in Galesburg.

As per the court, he will be eligible for his parole in Spring 2024.

The reports cite that his original projected discharge date is April 22, 2027.

He is not dead and serving his time in prison now.

Ashley Reeves Teacher Wikipedia Details

Sam Shelton is not on Wikipedia.

But his violent stories shredded upon the victim Ashley Reeves are all over the news portals and publications.

Similarly, Lifetime has brought a movie named “Left for Dead,” based on his case.

Sam Shelton Wife

Sam Shelton is not married, and he does not have a wife.

But he was in an alleged relationship with his student Ashley Reeves.

In an interview following Ashely’s missing, he mentioned that she was growing infatuated with him.

He said that he had hugged her to admitting sexual intimacy in the back of his vehicle. Authorities approached him after Ashley’s friends cited that they had a romantic fling going on at the time.

Shelton mentioned that he regretted the relationship with Ashley.
