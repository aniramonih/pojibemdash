Kanye West, Kim Kardashian Leaked Vogue Mirror Photo Sparks Funny Meme

Sweet Yeezus! Why does the Internet think Kanye West is suddenly a vampire? Vogue's April cover story continues to live on and one — thanks, at the moment, to a leaked interior photo shot by Annie Leibovitz and featuring the rapper, Kim Kardashian, and their 9-month-old baby North West.

Posted without official authorization on Monday, March 24, the arresting image features proud papa West, 36, snapping a photo of his stunning fiancee, 33, and daughter with an iPad — while Kardashian takes a selfie on her iPhone with Nori. The entire meta sequence was shot by Leibovitz in a nursery room with mirrored walls, which actually created one big problem: West's reflection is noticeably absent in the image!

Thanks to that apparent gaffe, the Internet has created a slew of hilarious memes over the last 24 hours — including one funny shot of Kanye looking at himself in the iPad. Vogue released additional authorized photos from its April 2014 issue Monday morning, including two sweet pictures of the engaged couple, and one adorable image with their daughter Nori.

"I put Kanye's big chains around her, and I put a little Louis bag and some Jordans, and I was like, 'What up, Daddy?'" Kardashian told Vogue of her daughter.
