Myspace Guy Tom Anderson, Your First Friend, Is Really Hot Now: Photo!

Is he still on your top eight friends section!? Myspace co-founder Tom Anderson, who is otherwise known as “Myspace Tom,” is really, really good-looking! The guy who became everyone’s first friend on the old-school website posted a rare selfie of himself via Instagram nearly two years ago — and it’s just making the rounds now.

Anderson, 44, helped develop the music and social networking site back in 2003 with Chris DeWolfe, but left in 2009. According to his about.me page, he’s now “retired and traveling the world taking photos” and can be found mainly stationed in L.A., Las Vegas, or Hawaii.

The guy with that pearly white smile often stays out of the limelight, and only shares professional scenery shots on Instagram. But 116 weeks ago, all of that (thankfully!) changed.
