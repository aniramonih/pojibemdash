Cristiano Ronaldo Jr Fame

He gained worldwide media attention after a short video of him showing his impressive skills with the ball went viral on the internet in June 2017, a couple of weeks before his seventh birthday.

Cristiano Ronaldo was accompanied by his mother Dolores Aveiro, brother Hugo Aveiro, girlfriend Georgina Rodriguez, and Ronaldo Jr. After parading with the UEFA Champions League trophy through the streets of the city, Real Madrid players had gathered at the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium to celebrate their 4-1 victory over Juventus in the Champions League trophy in Wales the previous day.

Towards the end of the ceremony, Ronaldo Jr., along with a few other kids, started to play with the ball. on a 20 sec long video captured by a fan, showed the young prodigy, clad in the club’s purple away jersey, picking up the ball in the penalty area and dribbling around two older children, beating them with a perfect pirouette, and finally rolling the ball past the goalkeeper into the bottom corner of the net.

The moment not only wowed thousands of fans present there, but the video was featured in many major news publications and also gained huge viewership on social media.
