Career

LeToya Luckett was a part of Destiny’s Child from 1993 to 2000. Their eponymous debut album was released in 1998. It was a commercially successful album and was released on Columbia Records. After her departure from the group, she released her self-titled debut album in 2006. It was a certified platinum album and released on Capitol Records. Her second album ‘Lady Love’ became available from August 2009. In 2017, she released ‘Back 2 Life’ on eOne.

She has also released many singles and has performed as a featured artist for Webbie, Ali & Gipp, Boney James, Jessica Simpson, and Mims.

In acting, Luckett was first seen in an episode of ‘Smart Guy’ in 1998. She starred as Alison Myers in the TV series ‘Treme’ in 2011. Her other television credit includes ‘Ballers,’ ‘Regular Show,’ ‘Single Ladies,’ ‘Greenleaf,’ ‘Here We Go Again,’ and ‘For Rich or Poorer.’

In films, Luckett has starred in ‘Lucky Girl,’ ‘Seasons of Love,’ ‘Drumline: A New Beat,’ ‘Addicted to You,’ ‘Until We Meet Again,’ ‘Preacher’s Kid,’ and ‘Note to Self.’
