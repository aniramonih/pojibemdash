Who is Cassie Bjorge? Teen Charged with Murder with Boyfriend Johnny Rider

If you remember The two name Cassie Bjorge and her boyfriend Johnny Rider were involved in murdering grandparents in 2017. Cassie’s grandparents were both around 63 years old at the time of death. When later police found the killers, she confessed how her boyfriend was also involved in murdering Wendy and Randall. Let us discuss more facts about both further in this blog.

Who is Cassie Bjorge?

Back in 2017 when she was involved in murdering her grandparents, Cassie Bjorge was 17 years old. She went to Duluth High School for her studies. Originally Cassie belongs from Atlanta, Georgia but later shifted to Lawrenceville.  Not much information about her personal life is known to anyone. Even the exact date of her birth was undisclosed. She was in a relationship with Johnny Rider who is 21 years old now.

They both planned to kill the family members reportedly Cassie’s grandparents,  mother, and Ride’s sister. The reason behind their cruel act was not disclosed to the public. After the case was registered with the police, they arrested and put both criminals behind the bars. Source like WSB-TV claims the teen was sentenced to life in prison.
