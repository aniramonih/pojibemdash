Courtney LaPlante Biography

Courtney LaPlante is a Canadian rock singer who was born and brought up in Alabama, Canada.  She rose to fame after assuming lead vocal duties for the metalcore band IWrestledABearOnce.     Courtney LaPlante Spiritbox

    Courtney and her hubby Mike Stringer came together in 2017 to form a band called Spiritbox. The band’s full lineup comprises LaPlante and Stringer and drummer Zev Rose. The band’s style is djent/metalcore and incorporates electronic elements.

    Dissatisfaction caused LaPlante and Stringer to exit their former band, Iwrestledabearonce, and they started Spiritbox, seeking artistic freedom. They released a debut extended play, Spiritbox (2017), followed by a second EP, Singles Collection (2019) with an expanded lineup.

    Rather than touring, Spiritbox initially developed an approach to fanbase building focused on the Internet and introduced their music through acclaimed music videos.

    The band began touring in 2020 and continued to gain popularity with several singles that reached the Billboard charts before releasing their debut album Eternal Blue (2021), which entered the US Billboard 200 at number 13. Their music is currently released through their label, Pale Chord, via a partnership with rising Records.
