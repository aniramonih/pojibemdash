How to get VALORANT recap for 2022 and check your Valorant stats: Year in Review Guide

As the end of the year approaches, many players of the popular first-person shooter game Valorant may be looking to take a look back at their performance over the past year. Fortunately, there are a few simple steps you can take to get a comprehensive overview of your stats and accomplishments in the game.

First, players will be required to visit the official website and log in to your account. Once you are logged in, click on the “Stats” tab in the top menu. This will bring up a page that displays your overall stats for the year, including your win/loss ratio, average kills and deaths per game, and your average ability usage.

Next, click on the “Matches” tab to see a detailed breakdown of your performance in each match you have played this year. This page will show you the specific maps, modes, and agents you played in each match, as well as your individual stats for each game.

If you want to see how you compare to other players, you can click on the “Leaderboards” tab to view the top players in various categories. Here, you can see where you rank in terms of overall wins, win percentage, average kills per game, and other important metrics.

Finally, you can use the “Achievements” tab to see the specific in-game achievements you have earned over the course of the year. These achievements are awarded for completing certain objectives or reaching certain milestones in the game, and they can be a great way to track your progress and see how you stack up against other players.

Players can get Valorant to send them their stats

If you want Valorant to send you the data, you’ll have to log into your Valorant ID and sign up. After logging in, you’ll have to link your email to the website and enable the communications tab to allow Valorant to send you your end-of-the-year review.

In conclusion, getting a Valorant year in review is a simple process that can help you see how you have performed over the past year and identify areas for improvement. By visiting the Stats, Matches, Leaderboards, and Achievements tabs on the Riot Games website, you can get a comprehensive overview of your stats and accomplishments and see how you compare to other players.
