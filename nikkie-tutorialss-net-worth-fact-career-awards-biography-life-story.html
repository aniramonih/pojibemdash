How was Nikkie Tutorials Career?

  • Nikkie started uploading videos to YouTube in 2008, at the age of 14, after watching MTV’s The Hills being inspired by Lauren Conrad’s makeup. 
  • After that, she began searching YouTube for tutorials to recreate the look and was inspired to begin creating her own.
  • After uploading videos for about two years, she enrolled in makeup coursework at B Academy in Amsterdam. 
  • She then signed to Colourfool Agency in 2011 and began working as a professional makeup artist.
  • She became the head makeup artist for RTL5’s show I Can Make You a Supermodel with Paul Fisher in 2013. 
  • She then left Colourfool Agency at the beginning of 2014 to work as a freelance hair and makeup artist.
  • In 2017, she won the award for “YouTube Guru” at the Shorty Awards and the award for “Choice Fashion/Beauty Web Star” at the Teen Choice Awards.
  • Moreover, Forbes magazine named her as one of the top ten beauty “influencers” in 2017.
  • She uploaded a video reviewing a high coverage foundation produced by a Prague-based cosmetics brand called Dermacol and spoke about the company using her image, specifically a screenshot from her “The Power of Makeup” video, to sell their products on social media without her permission in 2017. 
  • In January 2019, it was announced that she would serve as the Global Beauty Adviser for Marc Jacobs Beauty. 
  • In addition to this, she has collaborated with beauty brands Ofra, and Maybelline.
  • Her line with Ofra in 2017 included liquid lipsticks and a highlighter palette.
  • December 2019, she collaborated with Lady Gaga, promoting and giving away her makeup brand Haus Labs.
  • She competed on the Dutch television series Wie is de Mol? where she finished in ninth place out of ten in 2019.
