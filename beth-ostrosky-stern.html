Do Beth Ostrosky Stern Share Any Children With Her Husband Howard Stern? Know Her Married Life

If you have ever watched America's Got Talent, then Howard Stern must not be a new name to you. Today, we going to learn something about his present wife, Beth Ostrosky Stern. Along with being the wife of famous American radio and television personality, she is a model, author, and a animal-rights activist. 

Beth Ostrosky Stern was born on July 15, 1972, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Beth is the daughter of father, Robert Ostrosky, and his wife, Judy. Her father is a dentist, and her mother is a former model. She has two siblings. Beth has married to Howard for more than two decades. 

Beth is married to Man Eighteen years older to her

Beth and Howard tied the knot on October 8, 2008, at a private ceremony at the famed Le Cirque restaurant on East 58th Street as per New York Daily. The ceremony was attended by almost 180 guests, which included some renowned figures like Jimmy Kimmel, Donald Trump, and Barbara Walters.

Mark Conseulos officiated the wedding ceremony after the couple asked him for the procedure. After declaring the couple as husband and wife. Phoebe Snow sang her classic "You Send Me" to entertain the guests. 

The couple had been dating for more than eight years before walking down the aisle. The lovely couple disclosed the plan of their wedding in February 2007. The love birds are yet to welcome any child. 

How Rich is the Wife of Howard Stern?

Beth Ostrosky has a whopping net worth of $10 Million. The author has written one of the New York Times Bestselling books, Oh My Dog: How to Choose, Train, Groom, Nurture, Feed, and Care for Your New Best Friend. The book was listed in the fifth rank among paperback advice books. 

Beth's husband, Howard, has a net worth of $650 Million. Howard is the world's highest-paid radio host. He signed a five years deal of $500 million with Sirius XM in 2004. Along with that, he remained as the Judge of Americas Got Talent for four seasons from 2012 to 2015 and was replaced by Howie Mendel. 

The couple owns two houses in Long Island and Florida and an apartment in Manhattan. Howard bought a beautiful Villa in Florida back in 2017 for a staggering $52 Million. This home spreads over a area of 3.25 acres. 

Body Measurements and Modeling career

The forty-eight years old model came limelight after getting into a relationship with Howard. In the year, 2002, 2003,2004, and 2007, Beth was voted into FHM's 100 Sexiest Women of the Year by For Him Magazine. Then in 2007, she was placed at 96th in the list of the Most Desirable Women.

As Beth's mother was a model, she encouraged her daughter to pursue her career in modeling. During the early stage of her life, Beth started modeling and even tried acting, taking classes at the University of Pittsburgh. The actress has acted in a few movies like Flirting with Disaster(1996) and Whipped (2000). 

Beth's husband's previous marriage

Before getting married to Beth, Howard was married to Alison Berns. The couple were married in 1978 and welcomed three daughters; Ashley Jade Stern, Emily Beth Stern, and Deborah Jennifer Stern. 

However, the marriage did not last long and divorced in 2001. Soon after splitting with Howard, she tied the knot with David Simon.
