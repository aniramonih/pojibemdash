Who Was Don Brown?

Don Brown was a well-loved sports anchor from Dayton, Ohio, known for his friendly presence and dedication to the community. He had an impressive career in broadcasting, starting in 1979 at WHIO Radio and then transitioning to TV at WKEF-TV in 1985. Don quickly became the Sports Director in 1986 and held that position until 2002. Apart from his broadcasting career, Don was also deeply committed to education, coaching high school sports, and being an instructor at Stebbins High School.
