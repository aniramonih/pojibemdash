Taran Noah Smith Bio (Wiki)

Who is Taran Noah Smith?

Taran Noah Smith is an American former actor.

He is widely known as Marcus ‘Mark’ Jason Taylor in the longest-running and the 90s’ most widely views sitcom, ‘Home Improvement’.

Taran Noah Smith: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

He was born on April 8, 1984, in San Francisco, California, the U.S. His mother’s name is Candy Bennici and his father’s name is David Smith.

He has a sister named Ariandrea Hilary Smith who is an international model and he does not have any brothers.

His father belongs of Italian and Swedish descent while his mother belongs of English and Irish descent. He holds American citizenship.

Education, School/College University

He joined the University of Southern California and wanted to study film but he did not graduate as he dropped out later.

Taran Noah Smith: Professional Life and Career

Taran Noah Smith got a role in the popular sitcom, ‘Home Improvement’ before he was 10.

Also, he got a role in ‘ABC TGIF’ in 1990, in ‘Ebbie’ in 1995, in ‘Little Bigfoot 2: The Journey Home’, in ‘7th Heaven’ and in ‘Batman Beyond’.

He did not want to be an actor and he stayed away from the screen by the time he was an adult.

Taran joined SpaceX in the starting of July 2022 as an integration technician.

Awards, Nominations

He was awarded Young Artist Award in the category Exceptional Performance by a Young Actor Under Ten for ‘Home Improvement’ in 1992, and Young Artist Award in the category Outstanding Youth Ensemble in a Television Series for ‘Home Improvement’ in 1994.
