Hui Pentagon Real Name

Mostly, Music stars choose to have a stage name that is far from their birth name, Hui is not an exception. while some also coined their stage name from their birth name, Hugi has decided to look elsewhere for a new name purposely for his Music career.

Career

Hui’s talent when it comes to Music is one no one can have any doubts about at all, while many are good at only one Genre, He has excelled in different genres including K-pop, R&B, ballad and dance-pops.

The musician became much Popular during his days at Pentagon where he played a crucial role as a member.

He debuted As a member of the group through 2016 Mnet’s reality show Pentagon Maker on The 10th day of October in 2016. His instant impact gained him the status of becoming the leader of the group.

Fast Forward, Hui went straight to work, the Produce 101 Season 2 hit song, Never” was co-composed by him. He also played an integeral part in Wanna One’s debut single, “Energetic” alongside Flow Blow.

The song took the Music space by storm and  won many public votes as which song they would like to see for the group’s debut single.

Facts to know about Hui Pentagon
