Tommy Fury Height, Weight, Age, Girlfriend, Biography, Family &amp; More

Tommy Fury (born May 7, 1993) is a British Professional Boxer from Wythenshawe, England, United Kingdom. He is famous for being the younger brother of Tyson Fury (famous British Boxer and champion). Furthermore, the 19-year-old boxer is set to follow in the footsteps of his undefeated older brother.

On December 22, 2018, Tommy Fury will make his professional boxing debut in Manchester. Fury will fight against Jevgenijs Andrejevs (Latvian Professional Boxer) for the title. He has lost 102 times in his boxing career.

Tommy was trained by his uncle Peter Fury. In fact, his brother Tyson and cousin Hughie Fury also trained by Peter. Tommy started his training at the age of 8. During an interview, he also told his older brother also used to give him training.

Early Life, Childhood, Parents & Brother

The boxer was born on 7th May 1999 in Wythenshawe, England, United Kingdom. As of 2018, the age of Tommy Fury is 19 years old. His father “John Fury” was a former bare-knuckle fighter. Later, John turned professional boxer during the 1980s.

Furthermore, he has two brothers Tyson and Shane Fury. Tommy is a high school graduate but we have no information about his school and qualifications.

The 19-year-old boxer is currently in a relationship with his girlfriend Millie Roberts. She is a social media personality and model.
