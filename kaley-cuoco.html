Kaley Cuoco - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Nationality, Career

Kaley Cuoco is an American actress. Kaley Cuoco is famous for the portrayal of roles like ‘Penny’ on The Big Bang Theory, ‘Billie Jenkins’ on Charmed, and Bridget on 8 Simple Rules.

Early life and Childhood

Kaley Cuoco is 35 years old. She was born on November 30, 1985, in Camarillo, California, the United States. Her birth name is Kaley Christine Cuoco and birth sign is Sagittarius. She is the elder daughter of her parents. Her mother’s name is Layne Ann who is a homemaker, and her father’s name is Gary Carmine Cuoco who is a realtor. She grew up with her sister, Briana.
