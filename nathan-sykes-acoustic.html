Nathan Sykes unveils acoustic version of 'There's Only One Of You'

Nathan Sykes took to Twitter yesterday to announce that his next single from his album Unfinished Business will be ‘There’s Only One Of You’.

Whether you’re already familiar with the laid-back break up song or not, Nathan has since treated us all to a stunning acoustic version of the track.

‘There’s Only One Of You’ is the fifth official track to be released from his album and shows yet another side to the talented singer-songwriter. The song is stripped back compared to the rest of Nathan’s album, which is rich with big, brassy sounds. However, teaming up with a guitarist, this acoustic version of the song allows Nathan’s golden vocals to take centre stage.

Check it out below.

The new single announcement caps off a stellar year for Nathan. Having toured with Little Mix around the UK, and with Alessia Cara in the States, the singer has made his voice heard across the globe. He also released his highly anticipated debut album, Unfinished Business, which showed fans and critics alike, just what he was capable of.
