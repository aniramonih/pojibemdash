Career

Grand P gained public recognition during a show organized by his idol, Kerfala Kante, at the People’s Palace in Conakry. In 2019, he showcased his talent by supporting the Guinean delegation in Cairo. Post the CAN 2019, Grand P found himself in the company of stars like Oumou Sangaré, Affou Kéïta, Sidiki Diabaté, DJ Arafat, Naby Keita, Paul Pogba, who invited him to various concerts and football matches.
