Saint Alphonsus to close three urgent care clinics through Monday

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — Staffing shortages have forced Saint Alphonsus to close three local urgent care clinics across the Treasure Valley until Monday.

The hospital says it will close its clinics in Kuna, Nampa (Karcher Road), and its west Emerald location in Boise.

"We apologize for any inconvenience," the hospital said.
