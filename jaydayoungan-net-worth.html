JayDaYoungan Early Life

JayDaYoungan was born on July 15, 1998, in Bogalusa, Louisiana. His grandmother raised him as his mom served in the Army, whereas his father was sentenced to imprisonment throughout his growing years. His stepfather imbibed good traits in him and guided him a lot.

He initially wanted to be an NBA player and called Rajon Rondo his idol. However, his low grades made him unable to continue playing basketball while he was in high school. Around this time, he developed a passion for rapping and used to rap growing up. His friends motivated him to drop his first official single. In eleventh grade, he released his first single on YouTube. He then started performing in several clubs and after he began gaining recognition for his music, he dropped his studies in 12th grade. 

JayDaYoungan’s Career Before Net Worth

In 2016, JayDaYoungan started his musical career by releasing his debut tape, “YOUNGANimal,” The following year, he released “Ruffwayy.” It was until the release of his album titled “The Real Jumpman 23” which brought him immense fame. It consisted of a single like “Sliding Freestyle” and “Muddy Situation,” which received millions of views soon after its release. JayDaYoungan also rolled out some other projects like “Taking Off,” “Wake Up,” and “23.” 

He rose to prominence for his music; later, Cinematic Music Group signed him. His song titled “Elimination” from the album entitled “23” received the highest viewership. In 2018, he released his album entitled “Forever 23,” which had songs like “Purge” and “Thot Thot.” The album peaked at number 86 on the Billboard 200 and 37 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums. In 2019, he released a mixtape titled “Endless Pain,” which reached number 20 on the Billboard chart and 37 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop charts. His mixtape “Misunderstood” featured artists like YFN Lucci, Lil Durk, Lil Boosie, JetSoo, and FG Famous. 

In June 2020, he released his debut album titled “Baby23,” in which famous artists like Dej Loaf and Kevin Gates, among others. 

JayDaYoungan Legal Controversies

In 2019, JayDaYoungan was stopped by Florida Highway Patrol and charged with unlawful drug possession. He was then released against a bail worth $2500. The following year, his home was raided by cops and a cash of $24,000 along with other firearms and Oxycodone. He was then detained several times in 2021 for several criminal charges.

JayDaYoungan Death

On July 27, 2022, JayDaYoungan was shot outside his Louisiana home at 24. Reportedly, he was rushed to hospital in critical condition but couldn’t survive.
