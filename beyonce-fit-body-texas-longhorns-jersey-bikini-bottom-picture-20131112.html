Beyonce Reveals Fit Body in Texas Longhorns Jersey, Bikini Bottom: Pic

Lucky Longhorns! The University of Texas football team has a fan in Beyonce, as evidenced in new photos uploaded to the singer's Tumblr on Tuesday, Dec. 10.

In the pictures, Blue Ivy's mom, 32, reveals her super-fit physique in a white, string bikini bottom and the college football team's signature blazing orange jersey marked with David Ash's number 14, rolled up to expose her toned, flat tummy. (Texas announced in late November that 21-year-old quarterback Ash was out for the remainder of the season due to concussion-like symptoms.)

The images of the slimmed-down star were posted one week after Jay Z announced on his Life + Times website that he and his wife were going vegan up until Christmas Day. Beyonce received some flack last Thursday for wearing real fox fur to a vegan lunch in California. However, Tuesday's pics revealed the singer's appreciation for giraffes!
