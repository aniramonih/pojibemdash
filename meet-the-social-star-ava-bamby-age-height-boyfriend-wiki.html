Meet The Social Star Ava Bamby: Age, Height, Boyfriend, Wiki

Who is Ava Bamby?

Ava Bamby was born on 25 October 1996 under the zodiac sign of Scorpio, in Sofia, Bulgaria. She’s a 27-year-old notable cosplay and dance content creator, probably best recognized for gaining prominence primarily through her engaging content featured on her official TikTok account. She’s also known for sharing her provocative photos and videos on Instagram and other social media platforms.

How rich is she,? Ava Bamby Net Worth

As of the latter part of 2023, Ava’s net worth is estimated at over $1 million; acquired through her thriving internet-based career, which includes influencer marketing, brand endorsements, sponsored posts shared across her official social media accounts, and collaborations with fellow influencers and content creators.

Early Life, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity, Religion

Ava spent her early years in her hometown of Sofia until she moved to the United States in her teenage years. There is no any information about her parents since their names and professions haven’t been revealed to the public, however, her mother appears sometimes in her TikTok videos. She also keeps quiet about her siblings, so it is unknown whether she has any or if she is the only child of her parents. She holds Bulgarian nationality, and belongs to the White Caucasian ethnic group. She is of both Bulgarian and American descent. She follows the Christian faith.

Educational Background

Ava took an interest in the arts as a young girl ,and during her childhood attended acting classes, and attended a prestigious performing arts high school. During her time there, she became interested in learning languages – four by the time she matriculated. When she was 17 years old, Ava relocated from the US to Germany, and shortly after that moved to China to teach English. She eventually returned to the US to study at a military college, from which she graduated with Honors and obtained a Bachelor’s degree in International Relations with minors in Asian Studies, Mandarin, and National Security. Ava now speaks seven languages – Bulgarian, English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, Russian, and German. Speaking further about her education, she is also an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) and public safety diver, as well as recently obtaining her pilot’s license. At the moment, she’s attending a law school.

Rise to Fame on Instagram and TikTok

After graduating from a military college, Ava initially spent some time working at a local market in Washington, D.C. However, during the COVID-19 pandemic, she made the transition to full-time content creator for her official social media platforms, and launched her official Instagram account under the username ‘avabamby’ in late 2020; shortly after that, she became active on the highly popular TikTok application under the same nickname. Her content is mostly based on cosplay transformations, as she creates videos that capture her using costumes, accessories, and make-up to transform into different characters. She also frequently shares popular TikTok dance challenges while she’s dressed in her cosplay outfits. Besides that, she likes creating and uploading voiceovers and lip-sync videos to popular songs.

Due to her interesting content, she became one of the fastest-growing social media stars, amassing an impressive one million followers within her first 30 days, and surpassing three million followers in just four months. Her rapid growth on social media brought her amazing opportunities, but she’s also faced many alleged copyright violations, so she launched her own copyright protection company in 2021.

As of late-2023, Ava has amassed nearly 900,000 followers on Instagram, while she’s garnered over 5.5 million followers on TikTok, and her videos have been liked over 31.5 million times.

YouTube Channel

Over time, Ava expanded her influence on YouTube as well. Her official self-titled channel was created back in April 2018, but she wasn’t active on it until December 2022, when she uploaded her debut video under the title “My First Q&A”, in which she answered the questions submitted by her fans; the video has had nearly 190,000 views. She then started sharing short videos, and with more than 45, Ava has amassed over 11,000 subscribers and 450,000 views in total.

Other Social Media Platforms

In addition to her presence on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, Ava runs her official account, ‘@avabamby’ on the X app, formerly known as Twitter, on which she’s amassed nearly 720,000 followers. Besides that, she’s posting content on her official Facebook page, with more than 370,000 followers. Most recently, she created her account on the Fanplace service under the nickname ‘@avabamby’, through which she has direct interaction with her fans, providing exclusive content to those who purchase individual memberships for her channel. Furthermore, she has another Fanplace account through which she generously shares her content with her fans for free, which can be found by the nickname ‘@avabambyfree’.

Music Career

Apart from being a social media personality, Ava has also tried herself as a musician and singer. In 2022, she took a significant step by establishing her own production company, Bamby Music, and released three albums entitled “Blossoming”, “Seventh Heaven”, and “Escapism”, all of them available on Spotify and Apple Music.

Appearance and Vital Statistics

Ava has long light brown hair and dark brown eyes. She stands at a height of 5ft 1in (1.55m), weighs around 125lbs (57kgs) with vital statistics of 38-28-43, bra size 34D, dress size 8 (US), and shoe size 6 (US). Although it’s believed that she’s undergone plastic surgery, she denies it.

Personal Life

Ava isn’t very open about her personal life and tends to keep all the details about it away from the eye of the public. There is no information accessible in the media about her dating history or current boy (or girl) friend. Despite that, her fans believe that she’s been single for a while, and dedicated to her professional career rather than romantic relationships. Her current residence is somewhere in the United States; the exact location is unknown.

Trivia

She’s an avid animal lover, and her favorites are cats, even though she currently doesn’t have a pet.

Her favorite hobbies include listening to music, engaging in cooking, and capturing moments through photography.

She also enjoys watching documentaries.
