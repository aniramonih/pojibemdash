Kat DeLuna gets booed after singing national anthem in Dallas



20 year-old singer Kat DeLuna got booed after singing the national anthem at Monday night’s football game in Dallas before the Cowboys vs. Eagles match. As Randy would say, she had too many runs in the song and was just too “pitchy.” The crowd heard it and audibly booed her afterwards.

DeLuna had two moderately popular singles with guest artists: 2007’s “Whine Up” featuring Elephant Man and “Run the Show” with Busta Rhymes. Her debut and only album to date, 9 Lives, was out in August of last year and peaked at number 58 on the Billboard charts.

It sounds like she’s got some more work to do on her singing and attitude. It’s obnoxious to mangle the anthem like that.

Story via ONTD.
