Who is Jill Whelan?

How old is Jill Whelan?

As of 2024, she is 57 years old.

What is Jill famous for?

She is most famous for playing Vicki Stubing, the captain’s daughter, on The Love Boat, a role she portrayed from 1979 to 1986. She also appeared in the comedy film Airplane! in 1980.

What other TV shows has Jill appeared in?

Besides The Love Boat, she has made guest appearances on shows like Fantasy Island, Murder, She Wrote, and The Young and the Restless.
