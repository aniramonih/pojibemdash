What Chelsea Houska's Husband Does For A Living

Not only does Teen Mom 2's Cole DeBoer keep drama under control at home, but managing stressful situations is kind of his jam. According to Glossyfied, Cole works as a traffic control specialist. The outlet explained that this means he's "responsible for ensuring that roads and highways are in good condition at all times. The actual day-to-day work is pretty hands-on and may involve cutting brush that has grown along the roadside, removing obstacles on roads, placing snow way makers, and overseeing road maintenance workers." The role reportedly earns an average of $42K a year.

For the record, Cole's wife Chelsea Houska isn't the only one getting her entrepreneur on. Per In Touch Weekly, Cole launched his own sock line in 2017. And in 2019, he announced the Chelsea + Cole Collection with a company called Itzy Ritzy, writing on IG, "We're beyond excited to announce that we're collaborating with @itzyritzy on a collection of cool products for everyone and parents just like us!!!" The collection includes diaper bags and blankets.

His Instagram bio also mentions an account he and Chelsea share called Down Home DeBoers, which boasts about "bringing our farmhouse dreams to life." The page gives a look at the couple's home renovations, as well as a link to a wallpaper line. In other words, this Teen Mom 2 pair knows how to capitalize on their strengths and the spotlight, and their fans seem very here for it.
