Debbie Osmond Is Together With Her Husband, Donny Osmond For More Than 4 Deacdes

Debbie Osmond, aka Debra Glenn, is an American actress who is best known as the wife of singer-actor, Donny Osmond. She appeared in several movies, including the 1979 movies 'The Fir Tree' and 'Martha.'

Debbie was born on 26 February 1959 in Billings, Montana, USA. Similarly, she is of white ethnicity and holds an American nationality.

Married Life With Donny Osmond

The lovey-dovey couple, Debbie and Donny, tied the knot on 8 May 1978. Their wedding ceremony was held at the Salt Lake LDS Temple in Utah. As a matter of fact, Donny's father was against their marriage as he thought it would affect Donny's career, but they marry each other.

As of now, Debbie and Donnie Osmond have completed more than four-decade of successful married life without any kind of separation rumors.

Debby & Donny Wish Each Other On Their Wedding Anniversary

Yes, the married couple Debby and Donny wish each other on their wedding anniversary. On 8 May 2023, Donny said that it was the best decision of his life to ask Glenn to marry him.

Similarly, Donny shared pictures of him, and Debbie from their wedding on his Instagram on 8 May 2019 with a caption, "Happy 41st anniversary to my sweetheart, Debbie. The best decision I ever made was to share my life and heart with you." 

Previously, on 8 May 2016, Donny shared a picture of him and his wife on his Facebook account. The picture was of him and Debbie celebrating their 38th anniversary at Sundance in The Tree Room.

On the post, he posted two pictures, one 40 years ago, when they were celebrating Debbie's birthday. And the second one is in the same place after 4 decades.

Debbie Dated Donny's Brother Before Dating Him

Donny had his first encounter with his wife when she was only 15. During the time, she was interestingly dating her current husband's brother, Jay Osmond.

Donny later convinced her to be in a steady relationship with him after 3 years of hardship. And the rest is history.

The fourth son, Christopher, is also a married man who tied the knot to his wife, Alta Osmond. Like, his sibling, Brandon, he is also a father to three adorable children. Similarly, the fifth child, Joshua, is the only bachelor in the house.
