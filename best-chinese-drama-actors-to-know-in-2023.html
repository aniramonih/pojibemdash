Popular Chinese Drama Actors To Watch In 2023

Xiao Zhan

The actor and singer first gained public recognition as a member of the boyband XNine. He began his acting career in 2016 and became a certified hit actor with The Untamed (2019). The actor went on to give memorable performances in dramas like The Wolf (2020) and The Oath of Love (2022). His 2020 single ‘Spotlight’ set the Guinness World Record for the fastest-selling digital track in China. The actor has also partnered with major luxury brands like Tod’s, Gucci, Audi, Ralph Lauren, and Yves Saint Laurent as their spokesperson.
