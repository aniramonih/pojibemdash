Eka Darville is an Australian actor and voice artist who is known for playing Taylor in the children’s television series The Elephant Princess, Scott Truman/Red Ranger in the sci-fi action-adventure drama television series Power Rangers RPM, Adam Bridge in the television drama series Blue Water High, Malcolm Ducasse in the psychological thriller superhero detective television series Jessica Jones as well as in the superhero action drama miniseries The Defenders, Pip in the drama film Mr. Pip, Beau Morris in the psychological thriller television anthology series Tell Me A Story, Pietros in the historical drama television series Spartacus: Blood and Sand, Ryan Morgan in the musical drama television series Empire, and Diego in the fantasy supernatural drama television series The Originals and The Vampire Diaries. In addition to that, Eka Darville has also been cast in a variety of other shows and movies which include the likes of The Sapphires, East of Everything, Blue Water High, Bernard and Huey, Her Smell, Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes, Terra Nova, Answered by Fire, Power Rangers Samurai, and Shelter.

    Eka Darville Facts

  • In 2009, he was cast as Scott Truman/Red Ranger in the sci-fi action adventure show Power Rangers RPM. It serves as the 17th season of the television series Power Rangers.
  • Eka Darville made his first theatrical film appearance by playing the role of Hendo in the 2012 musical comedy-drama film The Sapphires, loosely based on a real-life 1960s girl group.
  • Along with Hugh Laurie, Xzannjah, Healesville Joel, and Kerry Fox, Eka Darville co-starred as a Victorian orphan named Pip in the drama film Mr. Pip in 2012. It is based on Lloyd Jones’ novel Mister Pip.
  • From 2013 to 2014, he appeared as Diego (one of Marcel’s guards and a member of Marcel’s inner circle) in the horror fantasy supernatural drama television series The Originals.
  • Eka Darville played the recurring role of Ryan Morgan (a documentary filmmaker who eventually becomes a fling of Jamal’s) in the musical drama television series Empire in 2015.
  • Alongside David Koechner, Jim Rash, Sasha Alexander, Richard Kind, Lauren Miller Rogen, Nancy Travis, Bellamy Young, and Mae Whitman, Eka Darville co-starred as Conrad in the drama/comedy film Bernard and Huey in 2017.
  • He brought to life the character of Ya-ema (Becky’s shaman and later convict) in the drama film Her Smell in 2018. His co-stars in the film included Elisabeth Moss, Cara Delevingne, Dan Stevens, Agyness Deyn, Gayle Rankin, Ashley Benson, Dylan Gelula, and Lindsay Burdge.
  • From 2019 to 2020, Eka Darville played the role of Beau Morris (a police officer who struggles to navigate his new reality after his adherence to a moral code blows up both his personal and professional life) in the psychological thriller television anthology series Tell Me a Story.
