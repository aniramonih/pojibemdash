Will Smith Makes Coachella A Family Affair With Jaden And Willow Jordyn Woods Approves

There’s nothing like making Coachella a family affair and that’s exactly what the Aladdin star did at the festival when he performed on stage with Jaden who had also performed with sister Willow. Mom Jada Pinkett-Smith was in the crowd during the festivities and there was no lack of support when the Smith men took the stage to perform Jaden’s hit “Icon”.

Will Smith shared a video from the moment on his official Instagram account that went viral and has over 7 million likes and more than 14,000 comments.

Will Smith is currently making a number of movies and stars in the Facebook watch series Will Smith’s Bucket List. It’s unclear if performing at Coachella with Jaden or making Coachella a family affair was on the bucket list, but even if it wasn’t it’s definitely a lifetime memory they will all cherish.

Will Smith captioned the Instagram video with “Done Did ‘dat! #coachella.” So maybe it was something he wanted to accomplish.

You may see a video of Jaden Smith and his sister Willow performing at Coachella in the video player below.

Longtime friend and former Kardashian BFF and insider Jordyn Woods gave Will Smith her seal of approval by commenting on the Instagram video with the word “Yesss!!”

The Smiths were quick to Jordyn’s defense when the drama went down involving Khloe Kardashian and her ex-Tristan Thompson. Jaden Smith also brought Jordyn Woods on stage previously showing that she has his unconditional love and support through this difficult time.

The Smiths always have Jordyn’s back and in return so does she.

Will Smith stated the following once he hit the stage with son Jaden.

“This is my first time on the Coachella stage, ain’t no way I’m going off like that. Run it one more time!”

Video footage of Will Smith’s performance has gone viral and the Smith family is getting a lot of love from fans. Many people also praised Jaden Smith for having such a great relationship with his dad that he shared his Coachella stage with him.
