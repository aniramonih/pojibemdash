Lord Infamous was an American rapper who was one of the co-founders of the hip-hop group Three 6 Mafia. Formed in 1991, the group has released multiple albums including Mystic Stylez, Chapter 1: The End, Chapter 2: World Domination, Choices, Da Unbreakables, Choices II: The Setup, Most Known Unknown, Last 2 Walk, and When the Smoke Clears: Sixty 6, Sixty 1. Moreover, Lord Infamous also worked as a solo artist and his discography consists of albums such as Lord Of Terror (Solo Tape), Futuristic Rowdy Bounty Hunter, Scarecrow Tha Terrible, Legendary Hits, King of Horrorcore, Back From Tha Dead (Deadly Proverbs), Legend, Scarecrow Tha Terrible Part 2, and The Man, The Myth, The Legacy.

Lord Infamous Facts

  • His career as a musician started in the late 1980s.
  • Other members of Three 6 Mafia have been DJ Paul, Juicy J, Gangsta Boo, Crunchy Black, and Koopsta Knicca.
  • Lord Infamous’ first solo album Lord of Terror was released in 1993 and it consists of songs like South Memphis, Gotta Make a Stang (ft. DJ Paul), Dedication, 9mm (ft. Juicy J), Murder On the Menu, Drag ‘Em From the River (ft. DJ Paul), and Scarecrow.
  • On October 23, 2007, he released his 2nd album The Man, The Myth, The Legacy.
  • Lord Infamous’s death was confirmed by DJ Paul to online hip hop magazine HipHopDX, stating: “He had a heart attack in his sleep. His mother found him dead this morning. He had been dead, the doctors say, for about five hours.”
