What is a nobody in Kingdom Hearts?

A Nobody is a dark creature that is created when a strong-willed individual becomes a Heartless in the Kingdom Hearts series. They are the empty shells left behind after the person’s heart is taken. Nobodies first appear in Kingdom Hearts Chain of Memories.

How do you become a nobody in Kingdom Hearts?

Nobodies are born when a Heart is swallowed by darkness and produces a Heartless. They are the remaining parts left behind by the heart: the body, giving a Nobody form, and the soul, giving the Nobody life. However, only those with a strong will such as Sora are actually able to continue on as Nobodies.

How is Roxas a nobody?

Roxas is a “Nobody” who was born from the main character Sora after he briefly lost his heart during the events of the first game. Roxas is a member of Organization XIII, a group of Nobodies who need him for his ability to wield the Keyblade, a weapon that allows him to capture hearts.

What is the difference between a Heartless and a nobody?

A Heartless is what happens when a Heart is overwhelmed by Darkness. Their Heart is lost and in the person’s place is a being of pure Darkness, aka a Heartless. A Nobody, on the other hand, is the body and mind of a person who has been turned into a Heartless. They lack a Heart but can over time regain one.

Who is the nobody in Kingdom Hearts 1?

The only Nobody that appears during Kingdom Hearts 1 is Xemnas, who is disguised as the Unknown. When Sora lost his heart, Roxas was born, but Roxas is not shown in Kingdom Hearts 1.

Why does Sora have a nobody?

Once Sora’s heart was gone, the body and soul left behind formed into a different being called a Nobody. His name is Roxas. Sora formed back into himself when he reunited with Kairi, but Roxas still remained.

How did Sora become a nobody?

With Sora being unable to seal the incomplete Keyhole, he decided to use the Keyblade of People’s Hearts on himself. This action released Kairi’s heart from Sora, reviving her, but it also completed the Keyhole and cast Sora into darkness, transforming him into a Heartless while creating the Nobody Roxas.

What are the 2 types of Heartless?

There are two types of Heartless: Pure and Emblem. The pure Heartless are born from the darkness found in people’s hearts and continue to consume them. Legends say that people’s hearts were originally pure and full of light until they began fighting over it.

Am I heartless if I don’t cry?

Myth #1 – If you don’t cry, you’re heartless. Although crying is a recognizable sign of sadness, it’s certainly not the only one. People express pain in many different ways, and just because you don’t outwardly show it doesn’t mean you feel nothing.

What does it mean when you’re a nobody?

If someone says that a person is a nobody, they are saying in an unkind way that the person is not at all important.

Who is Roxas’ love interest?

The ship between Roxas and Naminé, or Rokunami, is speculated to be Roxas’ love interest. It is derived from the connection and interactions between Roxas and Naminé throughout the Kingdom Hearts series.

Why didn’t Ventus become a nobody?

Ventus never became a Nobody because he never created a Heartless. Since he doesn’t have any darkness in his heart due to Vanitas, creating a Heartless was impossible. Ventus did form a connection with Roxas, as Roxas carries his heart within Sora.

Why can’t Roxas and Sora exist at the same time?

Roxas and Sora cannot exist at the same time because Roxas is Sora’s Nobody. When Sora became a Heartless, his body and soul took form as Roxas. However, with the use of a replica, Roxas can have a physical presence alongside Sora.

How does Roxas have a heart if he’s a nobody?

Roxas has a heart because when Sora became a Heartless, he released Kairi’s heart and revived her. However, Roxas is a special Nobody because he carries Ven’s heart within him, which allows him to have a heart and experience emotions.
