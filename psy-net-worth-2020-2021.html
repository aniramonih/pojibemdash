Early Life

Park Jae-sang, better known by the name of “PSY,” was born on December 31, 1977, in Gangnam District, South Korea, to his parents Kim Young-hee and Park Won-Ho.

He went to Banpo Elementary School and had also attended the Sehwa High School. He did not like school and used to be known as “class clown.” After completing his schooling, he went to the United States to attend Boston University. However, he dropped out and opted to go to Berklee College of Music.
