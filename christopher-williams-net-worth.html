What is Christopher Williams' Net Worth?

Christopher Williams is an American R&B singer and actor who has a net worth of $400 thousand. Christopher Williams was born in the Bronx, New York, and first gained widespread notice in the late 80s. He signed with Geffen Records and released such hit tracks as "Promises, Promises", "Talk to Myself", and "I'm Dreamin'". In the early 90s, he launched a moderately successful acting career, appearing in such projects as "New Jack City", "New York Undercover", "The Gunmen", and the cult hit, "Revenge of the Unhappy Campers".

He has also performed in multiple musicals. After releasing three albums, all of which charted on the Billboard 200, he took a break from recording from 1995 to 2001. He then released a fourth album, entitled, "Real Men Do", with Renegade Records. Over the course of his career, he has enjoyed four Top 10 hits on the US R&B charts.
