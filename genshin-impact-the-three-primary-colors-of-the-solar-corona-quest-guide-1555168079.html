Quest Unlocking Procedure:

To initiate the quest, engage in a conversation with Sluasi the Melusine situated in Fontaine City.

Initiating Location:

The quest commences at the Statue of the Seven, positioned in the northern vicinity of the Golden Apple Archipelago.

Locating the Colored Orbs:

The principal objective of this quest entails locating three distinct colored orbs within the designated area. These orbs can be found in the subsequent locations:

Red Orb:

Positioned on a diminutive isle towards the eastern side of the starting point.

Blue Orb:

Located on a petite island to the western side of the commencement location.

Yellow Orb:

Positioned on a small islet southward from the initial point.

Upon successfully identifying all three orbs, the subsequent step involves activating them in the precise sequence. The prescribed sequence for activation is as follows:

  • Blue Orb
  • Red Orb
  • Yellow Orb

Confronting the Adversaries:

Following the activation of the orbs, you will encounter a cohort of adversaries that necessitate overcoming.

Returning to Sluasi:

Subsequent to triumphing over the adversaries, proceed to return to Sluasi to culminate the quest.

Fruitful Completion and Rewards:

The completion of this quest warrants gratification in the form of Primogems, Mora, and supplementary items.
