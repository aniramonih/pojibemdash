Rosita Vasquez, Venezuelan Actress Died At 87

Recently saddening news has hit the internet. Rosita Vasquez, the popular  Venezuelan actress passed away. Social media websites like Twitter, Facebook are flooded with mourning messages. All over the platform her fan following shares condolences and tributes. The news of her sudden demise was confirmed on November 4, 2021. We will discuss the circumstances around her death later in this blog.

Who is Rosita Vasquez ?

Her full name is Rosa Margarita Angola Millán. She was born in  Isla Margarita,  Venezuela on  December 2, 1933. Rosa is well known for being the first television actress in the history of Venezuela. She began her acting career in 1953 with Anecdotario.

“Very good actress Rosita Vasquez, and that tone of voice that she enchanted, she listened to the novels and that incomparable voice equal to that of Josefina Guinand, on television she interpreted each role with great professionalism, may her soul rest in peace,” One said on Twitter. 

Later her popularity spiked many folds and she got ample opportunity to work in many television dramas. Her latest work in the field was in  Natalia del Mar.

Rosita Vasquez Death

The shocking news of Actriz Rosita Vásquez death left thousands of her fans following in utter disbelief. She was the role model for numerous newcomers in the acting field of Venezuelan. The information about her death was made public by her nephew.

One wrote mourning the death “For those who have not found out, today the Venezuelan actress with a long career Rosita Vásquez died in Tijuana, Mexico. For the novelists of the ’80s and 90s it is a painful game, but we will always remember it with affection and admiration. Light and peace to your soul.”

She died at the age of 87 years. At the time of her death, she was in Tijuana, Mexico.
