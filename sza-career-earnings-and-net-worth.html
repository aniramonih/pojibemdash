SZA career earnings and net worth

American singer-songwriter Solána Imani Rowe, born on November 8, 1989, is better known by her stage name, SZA.

She started recording music in the early 2010s and released two extended plays before signing with the hip-hop record label Top Dawg Entertainment, who helped her release Z (2014), her third EP and debut retail release.

She was born in St. Louis, Missouri. She co-wrote “Feeling Myself” with Nicki Minaj and Beyoncé in the same year. She was a featured performer on Rihanna’s song “Consideration” in 2016.

Ctrl, SZA’s debut studio album, received overwhelmingly positive reviews from music critics upon release in 2017.  The Recording Industry Association of America certified it triple platinum and it debuted at number three on the US Billboard 200.
