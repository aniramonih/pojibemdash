Dwayne Johnson Children: Meet Simone Alexandra Johnson, Jasmine Johnson, And Tia Giana Johnson: – Dwayne Douglas Johnson, popularly known as The Rock, is an American actor and former professional wrestler.

Dwayne Johnson was born on May 2, 1972, in Hayward, California to Rocky Johnson (father) and Ata Johnson (mother). He lived briefly in Grey Lynn, Auckland, New Zealand with his mother’s family where he played rugby and attended Richmond Road Primary School before returning to the U.S.

Dwayne Johnson grew up with 2 other siblings; Curtis Bowles and Wanda Bowles. He has a great physique, he stands at a height of 1.96 m and has a weight of 118 kg.

Dwayne Johnson is a happily married man. He has been married on 2 different occasions. Johnson first met Dany Garcia in the early 1990s. They got married on May 3, 1997.
