Beaver Moon 2023 Horoscope: What signs will be more affected by the next full moon?

The Beaver Moon, influenced by the communicative and inquisitive nature of Gemini, serves as a celestial spotlight on hidden aspects, urging individuals to release what hinders personal growth.

Aries is prompted to reconsider friendships and redefine their chosen family. Taurus is advised to assess material possessions and career satisfaction, suggesting a budget reevaluation.

Meanwhile, Gemini itself is encouraged to embrace both light and darkness, recognizing hindrances to success. Cancer, strongly impacted by full moons, is urged to rekindle faith, close past chapters, and live fully in the present.

Leo experiences an exciting time, with the full moon activating their 11th house, encouraging them to connect with inspiring people and take steps towards their aspirations.

Virgo's talents are recognized as the full moon lights up their 10th house, boosting confidence and closing the gap between current status and career goals.

Libra explores belief systems in the ninth house, potentially involving travel or career change. Scorpio delves into intimacy and endings, breaking barriers for love to find its way. Sagittarius shifts from pessimism to passion, inviting romance and positive changes.
