Ryu Kai Her Loses Life in Sacto By Night Lounge Shooting

In a terrifying incident that unfolded in the south Sacramento neighborhood, two individuals tragically lost their lives in a shooting at Sacto By Night Lounge. The event took place in the 7000 block of Governors Circle.

The Victims

One of the victims was the 23-year-old Ryu Kai Her, who had arrived at the club to pick up his cousin and friends. The other victim was the owner of the nightclub. Both individuals were pronounced dead at the scene.

The Cause of the Incident

The unfortunate event was sparked by an altercation in the parking lot involving a gang that had overstayed after the nightclub’s closing time. In the ensuing brawl, a member of the gang opened fire, hitting both victims.

The Perpetrators

The culprits involved in the shooting were later apprehended by agents from the California Highway Patrol. More details about the suspects have not been revealed, as the investigation is ongoing.

Remembering Ryu Kai Her

Born in Stockton, Ryu Kai Her was a graduate from Cesar Chavez High School. He was an employee of the Stockton Unified School District. Those who knew him are mourning the tragic loss of a young life cut short by a senseless act of violence.

Community Response

The community has been left in shock following the incident. There is a collective call for action to address the issue of gang violence, which has led to such a tragic loss of life.

The police continue to investigate the incident thoroughly, and more information will be provided as it becomes available. The hope is that justice will be served swiftly for the victims and their families.
