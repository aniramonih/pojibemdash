Brittany Family Are Also Part of Entertainment Industry

Brittany's father is Leif Bristow, a director, producer, and writer. The father-daughter duo has worked together in several movies, including "Sophie &Sheba," and "Love on Safari."

Brittany and her father Leif have worked together in various movies. SOURCE: Leif Bristow Instagram (@leifbristow)

Bristow expressed that she enjoys working with her father but also feels the pressure to work harder when he is directing. She wants to show people she is in the movie because she deserves and not because of any favoritism.

The star's mother, Agnes Bristow is an accomplished woman as well. She is a social worker, a real estate agent, a painter, a woodworker, a producer, and a published author. She produced the television series "Wildlife Quest" alongside her husband and daughter.

Brittany's parents were supportive of her career from early on. SOURCE: Brittany Bristow Instagram (@britbristow)

Brittany's parents were always supportive. She decided to do acting at the age of six, and they respected her decision. They made time for her and used to drive her to various auditions.

Bristow's parents though were helping in her career; they made clear that they are doing so only because it is making her happy. If at any point of time she wants to change her directions, they will be satisfied with that decision also.

The 29-year-old actress family also includes a big sister. There is a significant age gap between them. From her sister, she has a niece.
