The pair reconciled before the trial and got married in 2019 in London. Nabilla gave birth to their son named Milann in the same year.

    Nabilla Benattia Facts

  • Her parents had separated when she was 13 years old and she spent a few months in South Korea after that.
  • Nabilla was first hired by a modeling agency when she was just 14 years old.
  • In 2009, she was arrested at the Geneva airport for being a party to a fraud alongside 3 Congolese men and was sentenced to 6 months in jail. Her charges pertained to opening bank accounts using a false identity and cheating older people out of their money.
  • In 2011, she was elected “Miss Salon international de l’automobile de Genève” while working on the Peugeot stand at the Geneva International Motor Show.
  • She has admitted to misquoting her age to participate in the French reality TV show l’Amour est aveugle. She was just 17 years old at that time.
  • Nabilla became popular in France after the phrase ‘non, mais allô quoi’ (No but hello, what) spoken by her on the reality TV show Les Anges de la télé-réalité went viral. The one-liner was used in advertisements by brands such as IKEA and Carrefour. Due to the popularity of the one-liner, she got it registered as a trademark at the Institut National de la Propriété Industrielle.
  • In an interview, she revealed that she underwent br*ast enlargement surgery to appear in magazines such as Maxim and Playboy.
  • In 2014, Nabilla worked as a columnist for Touche pas à mon poste!, which is considered to be France’s most popular talk show.
  • In 2016, she launched an autobiographical book titled Trop Vite.
  • In 2018, Swiss poet and conceptual artist Andrea Picci unveiled a series of re-invented classical paintings, including the Mona Lisa, on to which he had photoshopped Nabilla’s physical features.
